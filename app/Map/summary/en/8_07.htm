EFFECT Beresteyska Union DEVELOPMENT OF THE CHURCH LIFE IN UKRAINE

Union of Brest worsened the Orthodox church, but it remained true to the majority of contemporary Ukrainian population.

Deploying the struggle for restoration of rights oppressed Church, which joined representatives from all segments of Ukrainian society, and led their fraternity.

Broad powers Brotherhood authorizes stavropegic that he got some of them from the eastern patriarchs. Note. Stauropegic - Autonomous Orthodox unit (church, monastery brotherhood), which is not subject to the jurisdiction of the local bishops, but directly to the Patriarch, and enjoys special rights.

Stauropegial except Lviv Dormition from 1620 and Holy Cross were Kyiv Epiphany Brotherhood.

Right stavropegic was extremely important for the preservation of the Orthodox Church. In fact, after the Union of Brest almost all Orthodox bishops were Uniates. Orthodox Faithful were only two bishops in Lviv Gideon Balaban and Michael Peremyshl Kopystyansky. After their death, the only Orthodox bishop was Jeremiah Tysarovskyy who managed to occupy Lviv department, assuring the King that he - Uniates. After the death of Jeremiah Polish government could use the absence of bishops, the Orthodox Church to declare non-existent.

Convert fraternities into a powerful force that can fight for the Orthodox faith, was gradually. At the beginning of the XVII century. Brotherhood - a very influential religious organization. For example, they were entitled to because of their priests put out their parishioners from the church, the priests observe compliance with regulations and so on.

Activity Orthodox brotherhoods took care of the Cossacks, who were among many brothers. The Cossacks supported the Brotherhood and the Orthodox Church not only money, but also by military force. With the support of the Cossacks in 1615 in Kyiv Epiphany Brotherhood was founded, and joining him Peter Konashevich Sahaydachnyi with all the Cossack Army showed that the Cossacks took the Orthodox faith under care and protection.

It is with the support of the Cossacks in 1620 was changed higher Orthodox Church hierarchy.

The Greek Catholic Church

Position

Uniate bishops, contrary to what our Polish authorities did not receive the same rights that they had the Catholic clergy, so to achieve equality of churches, supporters hoped the union, they could not.

To the Ukrainian Uniate Church, most of the population belonged to someone else as formed by the treachery of their ancestral faith. In addition, the power of the Commonwealth to support her in the fight against the Ukrainian Orthodox Church.

The situation around the Greek Catholic Church tense and property disputes, as Uniates and the Orthodox churches and monasteries all Greek rite believed her.

Hierarchs Greek Catholic Church resorted to vigorous measures to overcome the crisis of his church.

Potiy Hypatia, who was called "the father of the union," the death of Michael cattail, became metropolitan.

He was a talented preacher, polemicist, administrator, however, in the struggle for union operated mostly by force.

Metropolitan Joseph-Velyamin Rutsky worked very hard to build the Uniate Church.

Unlike its predecessor - Poti Rutsky particularly concerned about the increasing level of education of the clergy, aggressively sought to come to terms with the Orthodox, seeing the rise of the main conditions of the consent of both churches.

He also launched activities aimed at streamlining and updating of church life, laid new monasteries, implemented strict discipline among the monks, took care of establishing the Uniate seminary, although it is open enough for funds.

Reformed the Uniate monks, which were combined into one order - Basilian. Like the Jesuits, the Basilian engaged in educational and missionary activities.

Restoration of the Orthodox hierarchy. METROPOLITAN Job Boretskyi

To restore the higher the Orthodox hierarchy in 1620 put a lot of effort Job Boretskyi.

Great Expectations in the revival of the Orthodox Church, Metropolitan Boretskyi placed on education and typography.

As the founder and patron of Kyiv Brotherhood school, he supported its own expense.

Job Boretskyi rallied around a constellation of talented scientists, educators, including Meletius Smotrytsky, Peter Graves, Lawrence Zizaniya, Elisha Pletenetsky.

A relatively associates Metropolitan Cossack leaders were Peter Konashevych-Sahaidachny Michael Doroshenko, Olyfer Golub. In the fight against the union Boretskyi relied primarily on the Cossacks.

Events Peter Graves on updating church life

Introduced permanent supervision of clergy discipline, order of worship and more. Must it was announced priests educational sermons to the congregation on Sundays and holidays.

Increasing the number and content update church books.

Property and the land returned to the Ukrainian Orthodox Church.

Restoration of Orthodox sacred places.

Increasing cultural and educational level of the Orthodox priests.

Values ​​of Peter Graves, aimed at building religious and cultural life of Ukraine, it is impossible to overstate. No wonder the time during which he was the Metropolitan, called Mohyla era. Kyiv Metropolis by Peter Graves was the most independent and authoritative.
