Year 1764 last hetman Kirill Razumovsky was denied mace. And events meant the final abolition of Hetman. Soon the king's government abolished the Cossack regiments Hetman Slobozhanshchina. Came the turn and to Zaporozhye. In 1775, Russian troops destroyed the Sich and captured all of Zaporozhye. That was done away with the remnants of the independence of Ukraine. By the time they underwent redistribution Ukrainian lands that were part of the Commonwealth, many of them became part of Russia.

In 1783 the Russian Empire had annexed the Crimea.
