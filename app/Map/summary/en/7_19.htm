1. The political divisions of Kievan Rus' and Galicia-Volyn state

Duke Power

The greatest power in Kievan Rus' and Galicia-Volyn state were princes.

Princes care law and justice, led the army, has taken a decision to defend the country, taking a direct part in military campaigns.

They forged relations with neighboring countries, concluded peace or declared war.

Thus, using modern terminology, we say that the prince of Rus lands was head of state; it embodied a quiet, ordinary life of society.

Princely power passed from father to son, that it was hereditary.

Of all the princes of Kyiv prince had the greatest power; rest of the princes obeyed him as the oldest, most. Such relations are called mizhknyazivski vasalitetom, and form of government - monarchy.

The political divisions of Kievan Rus' and Galicia-Volyn principality researchers define as federal- monarch.

In addition to the princes, the state power in the Ruthenian lands carried Council of the prince, Prince conventions, rallies.

With the strong role of the prince's power was formal; respectively, when the princely power was weaker, the council won more weight.

Thus, the important role played boyar council Galician principality.

However, no matter how influential were the knights of the council, they would prefer to have a let and obedient, but still a prince.

Congresses of princes

Grave authority had princely congresses. They gathered regularly and not all solutions of such gatherings have been realized, but the issues they discussed at the congresses were mostly of national importance.

Particularly sensitive issue that was discussed at the princely congresses, was the protection of Polovtsian. Here princes showed determination and unanimity.

2. The social structure of Kievan Rus' and Galicia-Volyn state

At the top of the social pyramid Russian society were princes.

Princes occupied the greatest power in the state.

Prince in the Ruthenian lands could only be representative of the family dynasty.

The head of state and influential Duke was Grand Prince of Kyiv.

In the capitals of the lands, principalities sat princes. Each principality, in turn, divided into small districts, which also ruled by princes.

Only son of Prince could be a prince.

In addition to the princes, to nobles ruling class.

Boyarsky formed with the families of local tribal elite and influential men, who by military service received from the princes of the land estates.

Together with the princes, nobles were ruling the state, the Principality.

Great nobles became rulers, rulers of thousands, and small - sotskymy, tens, tiunamy (bailiff), butler, etc.

Privileged social group Russian society arms - professional soldiers.

They were not only in military campaigns, but also covered some government positions.

For faithful service to Prince warriors were given a cash prize, and land.

To the privileged classes and include clergy.

The clergy were the most educated stratum of contemporary society.

Clergy divided into top (metropolitan bishops, abbots of monasteries) and the ordinary priests.

A significant role in the lives of the monks of the monasteries.

The middle of the social pyramid sat wealthy urban elite, and merchants and artisans.

At lower levels of the social pyramid Russian society: stinks, people, shopping, ryadovychi, servants, laborers, slaves, outcasts.

The largest group of the then population - peasants stinks. They were personally free, have their own farm land, lived in villages and princely prince paid tribute.

Peasants in written sources called simply "people" - mostly in rural communities.

If serf some circumstances lose their own farm, he could borrow money - "a bunch" in the feudal time, but had to work out a loan. Thus, a farmer who worked for "a bunch" in the enterprise master called «shopping».

After working for debt, a peasant become temporarily dependent free.

A variety of shopping were ryadovychi, because the circumstances of the transformation shopping smerds were fixed in the contract-series. It interprets the name "ryadovych."

Many Rus used the name "hireling", "Chelyadyn."

If hired to work in the conditions of employment, being personally free, the servants were not free - it is sold, given away, passed by inheritance.

Servants fell mostly prisoners.

Wholly owned by Mr. and were slaves.

A special group of the population were outcasts. Names of people who for various reasons dropped from his usual environment, lost contact with him.

3. Forms of tenure

Place in the social strata pyramid defined largely by their right to land.

Main landowners while the princes.

Grand Prince of Kyiv was considered the main owner of the land.

Dependent on his princes possessed by individual states.

Even smaller territories ruled Township princes and nobles.

Significant land estates had Orthodox Church.

In lands that were privately owned, has remained constantly being reduced, a small part of the land that they owned rural communities.

Gradually happened patrimonial estates, which involved a consolidation of those areas or those with a specific branch of the princely family.

Standards otchynnoho tenure endlessly excited by force of arms.

Researchers claim that the Kievan Rus' and Galicia-Volyn state had two main forms of feudal tenure: conditional - estates and unconditional - paternal.

For centuries they coexisted, although their share in different historical periods was different. In the first centuries of history dominated the landed gentry; during the disintegration - patrimonial.

4. feudal obligations

The burden of feudal obligations lay on the shoulders of peasants.

Working on the land of the landowner, or they had some time to work on feudal lord, or give him some food produced in your own house, or pay tax money.

Another form of duties - refining.

In addition, the rural population was forced to participate in the construction of cities and fortifications.

5. The economic life

The leading occupation of the Russian population was agriculture.

Prominent occupied Farming.

Addition of rye and wheat, Russians farmers planted various vegetables: cabbage, turnips, cucumbers, pumpkins, garlic, carrots, beets, onions.

Nurtured our ancestors and gardens, preferring apple, pear, plum, cherry.

An important branch of agriculture was animal: Rus bred cattle, pigs, sheep and horses. In agricultural work used horses.

Become widespread and agricultural industries: Hunting, fishing and beekeeping. In those times there were even some villages, residents are specialized in a particular fishery.

According to researchers in the 13th century. in Kievan Rus craftsmen knew no less than 78 specialties.

Leading industry handicraft production was iron mining.
