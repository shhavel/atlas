PERSONALITY

Real name Peter Konashevich Sahaydachnyi - Konashevych. Sahaidachny - a nickname that comes from the word quiver - leather bag or wooden case for arrows. Sahaidachny Peter Konashevich called for exceptionally skilful archery. Even one of his portraits by contemporaries, he was depicted with a quiver on his back.

On childhood and youth Peter Konashevich Sahaydachnyi remained extremely scanty information. Born between 1577-1578 biennium. In. Kulchytsi Sambora near Lviv region in a family of small Ukrainian Orthodox nobleman. Got primary education at home, and later studied at the Ostrog Academy and the Lviv Brotherhood school. At the end of the XVI century. went to Kharkiv, where he quickly gained credibility. He participated in numerous campaigns Cossacks, and soon became their lead. Had the reputation as an outstanding statesman. He Hetman 1617 Zaporozhian Host was included in the European league to fight against the Porte. Aiming to create a powerful anti-Turkish alliance, a leading role Hetman placed on the Cossack republic, he established diplomatic relations with Moscow, Georgia, Iran. A brilliant military leader, a wise politician, a talented diplomat Peter Konashevych-Sahaidachny earned a well-deserved tribute to his contemporaries.

Political, military and educational activities PETER Konashevich Sagaydachniy

Merit Hetman:

1. order the Cossacks.

For the first time established a disciplined Cossack army, armed to the last Cossack guns and the then artillery.

Achieved growth quantitative composition of Zaporozhye fleets, in which the number of seagulls - the main combat units - has reached several hundred.

2. He had considerable diplomatic talent.

Thanks peaceful diplomatic measures Sahaydachnyi respected at the royal court - for the first time Poles were dealing with a reputable Cossack leaders with whom you can negotiate and who was able to keep in check the Cossack element.

This situation was in favor of the Cossacks, under conditions of constant threats by Turkey, Poland did not dare to destroy the Cossacks - its ally in the struggle against the Turks.

Cossack Council decided to act jointly with the Poles, provided the Polish government a number of requirements, including:

The course of hostilities

When Khotynska fortress (now Chernivtsi region) agreed 35,000th polish and 250-thousand Turkish-Tatar army. Polish command was looking forward to the Cossack units.

Soon came from Warsaw Sahaidachny. He was elected hetman.

Under the direction Sahaydachnyi Cossacks recaptured nine assaults, made several night attacks, except one, were successful.

The main burden of the war fell on the Ukrainian Cossacks.

P`yatytyzhnevi fights, in which a crucial role was played by the Cossack infantry, ran for the Turkish-Tatar forces defeat. September 29, 1621 between the Commonwealth and Turkey signed a peace treaty.

Implications and significance

Thanks Khotyn war Poland diverted danger of losing a lot of areas.

The first high-profile victory over the Turkish army received in upland battle, was of great international importance. She refuted the idea of ​​invincibility Turkey and caused an outbreak of the liberation struggle of the peoples conquered by the Turks.

The Old War changed the foreign policy plans of the Ottoman government, since Turkey for a long time refused intend to conquer all of Europe.

Affected by War and the domestic situation of the Ottoman Empire: angry defeat Janissaries killed Sultan Osman II. This rebellion was the harbinger of the decline of a powerful empire.

Thanks to the heroic actions of Khotyn war Cossacks gained general acceptance in Europe as a brave and courageous soldiers who have skillfully art of war on land and on water.

Zaporozhian Host increasingly serving as a factor in international politics, as the vanguard of the national liberation struggle of the Ukrainian people.
