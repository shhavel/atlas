In general, the eastern Diaspora was formed under the influence of not only economic factors. Mostly Ukrainian migration to the east was carried out by the Russian Empire, and later - the Soviet Union for political reasons. In this case, Ukrainian immigrants were not in the traditional sense of the word, because many of them were forcibly relocated (as, for example, during the collectivization). Using the inherent Ukrainian diligence, the central government decided several tasks: check-freedom-loving members of the ethnic group of the ancestral territories of local ethnic assimilation and development of new uninhabited land (Siberia, the Far East, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan).

Settlers from Galicia and Transcarpathia shaped western vector Ukrainian emigration (USA, Canada, Brazil). The governments of these countries in 70-90-ies of the XIX century. encouraged mass migration of workers from abroad. In 1877 the first group of Ukrainian emigrant settlers arrived from the forests to the United States, added to the bench miners of Pennsylvania. But most Ukrainian peasants sought to obtain for agricultural cultivation of land - homsted (allotment that was granted funds from public land on favorable terms in the United States and Canada to those who wanted to keep the farm).

At the end of the XIX century. slightly less than the number of was a Ukrainian immigrant communities in Canada. The vast majority were Galician and Ukrainian Bukovina. They settled in deserted places in Western Canada, where he received homsted.

In newly built settlements Ukrainian immigrants retain national traditions - and especially housing, and arts and crafts (furniture, dishes, clothing). Just celebrating weddings, christenings, funerals, celebrated national and religious holidays, do not forget folk songs brought from the homeland, and create new ones.
