General Information

It lies in the north of Ukraine on the left bank of the middle reaches of the Dnieper, the Desna River basin. Surface area - mostly low-lying and flat, sometimes - polohohvylyasta plain bowed to the southwest. It lies in the Dnieper Lowland. In the north-eastern region common Karst (funnel karst abyss). The south-east - increased hilly undulating portion located within the Poltava plains (100-220 m). It is dominated by erosion-accumulative landforms (gullies, ravines) in combination with accumulative ice and water-ice ridges. On sandy terraces - aeolian landforms (ridges, hills, dunes). In the floodplain of the Dnieper, the Desna River and its tributaries swampy area covered by peat bogs. In Chernihiv are a variety of minerals. Are important fuel - oil and natural gas (the northern part of the Dnieper-Donets oil and gas field). The most significant deposits of peat - in Repkinsky, Chernigov and Semenov areas. Historical Background

In the VII-VIII centuries. on the territory of Chernihiv Region East Slavic tribes lived northerners. From the X century. Chernihiv-Siver land was part of Kievan Rus'. In 907. Appear first mention of Chernigov. Since 1024 the city became the center of Chernigov principality, and the size and importance second only to Kyiv. This is evidenced by the many monuments, together with Kiev temples became the golden fund of Ukrainian architecture. In 1239 Chernihiv region was destroyed and burned the Mongols. Later Chernihiv was in possession of Lithuania - the XIV century. Muscovy - from the beginning. XVI., Poland - early. XVII. After the National Liberation War of 1648-1654. Chernigov lands administratively divided into 4 shelves that existed before 1775 Hetman was the seat of the city. Baturin. At the time of the Great Northern War the city was completely burned by Russian troops (in 1708). In 1802 was founded the Chernigov province. Great losses suffered Chernihiv during the Second World War, but was able to rise from the ruins, as so many times over the centuries of its existence.
