1. Origin of the name Rus

It was called "Rus" know first state in our country strangers. So he called her and chronicler.

But now as to the origin of the name "Russia" among scientists there is no unanimity.

Many researchers believe the word is Scandinavian in origin and brought to our land Scandinavians, Vikings. This opinion on the name "Rus" followed, and chronicler.

In particular, he spoke of soldiers from the north - Rousseau-Normans, who seized power in Kiev, established themselves in the city and gave its name to the local population and the territory in which the ruled.

In addition to the testimony of the chronicler, proponents of Scandinavian origin of the name "Russia" refer to a foreign source, none of which mentions the Slavic tribe Rousseau, although the names of, say, Derevlyany or northerners they certify.

For Scandinavian origin ethnonym "Rus" and serves many linguists.

However, this evidence refutes those researchers who believe that the name "Rus" local East Slavic origin.

They argue their beliefs in the Middle Dnieper large number of ancient names of rivers from the root -ros- that he deduce from -rus- like: Ros Rostavytsya, ROSAVA more.

Earth where these rivers flow, supporters of Slavic origin of the name "Russia", according to the territory of eastern Slavic tribe Rousseau.

What tribe Rousseau is not mentioned in the chronicles and other sources, they explain the ignorance of ancient authors or their inaccuracy.

From the chronicle name of the state centered in Kiev - Russian land - historians, scientists have created a bookish name "Kievan Rus", which is now in general use.

Campaigns against Byzantium

According to the chronicles, year 907, Oleg led an expedition against Byzantium. The story chronicler of the brilliant victory of the legendary Oleg full details and details.

But the greatest value to historians is listed in the "Tale of Bygone Years" into the contract between Rusichi and Byzantines.

Terms of the agreement were favorable for Russia.

Byzantium at the mercy of every pay Rus warrior UAH 12 and a considerable tribute to the Russian princes and influential Duke East Slavic tribes who obeyed Oleg.

For Ruthenian state recognized the right to trade with Byzantium without paying customs duties.

The Byzantines were obliged to hold n merchants in its capital - Constantinople for six months and still provide all the necessary equipment for the return journey.

Terms and conditions 907. Were specified in 911. - Oleg after the new campaign.

Making Oleg partnership agreement with Byzantium was an exceptional value, as demonstrated international recognition of the Russian state. The agreement drew a line under that period of the history of the Kievan state, which it is called formation. This is why scientists have used the name "Kievan Rus" is from the early 10 century.

4. The Board of Prince Igor

Unlike its predecessor trustee and the Principality of Oleg, the chronicle stories of which still prevails legendary Prince Igor - very real historical figure.

Having gained power after the death of Oleg in 912 g. Son of Prince Rurik ruled in Kiev to 945.

As Oleg, he had to deal with disobedience princes subject to tribes.

Year 914, Igor lucky Derevlyany rein and impose on them a tribute greater than Oleg.
