Religion is an important part of the spiritual life of humanity, which largely determines the formation of mentality and values ​​of individual nations, but also affects the political and socio-economic situation of entire countries and regions.

The most common and most influential religions in the world are Christianity, Islam and Buddhism. They are called world religions. Besides the world have national religion, ethnic characteristics of certain nations (Hinduism, Judaism, Shinto, etc.).

Most numerous the number of believers is Christianity that there 2000 years. Christian faith professes, nearly a third of the world population, mainly in Europe, America and Australia. Christianity is divided into three trends: Catholicism, Orthodoxy and Protestantism. Currently 60% of all Christians in the world are Catholic. Only the center of the Catholic Church is the Vatican, where the permanent residence of the head of the church - the Pope.

Orthodoxy - current Christianity that formed after the division of the churches in 1054. In the Orthodox faith, there is no single steady center, instead, autocephalous national churches, led by patriarch.

Among the supporters of Protestantism stand out: Lutherans, Anglicans, Baptists, Adventists, Mormons and others.

The second largest number of believers and the youngest world religion - Islam is also not uniform. Followers of Islam (Muslims) are divided into Sunnis and Shiites. The Sunnis, in contrast to the Shiites, along with the Koran - the holy book of Islam - the source of faith believe Suyu - the sacred writings of the prophet Muhammad.

Third the number of believers world religion - Buddhism formed of supporters narrow path (Hinayana) and fans of the great path (Mahayana).

The largest by number of adherents of religions are Hinduism. It has more than 5000 years and finds polytheism. The main Hindu gods - Brahma, Shiva, Vishnu, Kali.

Judaism originated in Palestine 4 thousand years ago. Jews believe in one God, whose grasp of the great revelations of the prophets. At the time, this belief gave rise to Christianity and Islam.

Chinese is the national religion of Confucianism and Taoism. The ancient cults of nature and ancestors, the Japanese took shape in modern Shinto.

The traditional local beliefs, the so-called primitive religion professed by the aborigines of Australia, some Asian and African tribes, American Indians, the people of northern Russia.
