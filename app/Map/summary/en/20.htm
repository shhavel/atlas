In the maelstrom of World War II Ukrainian lands were involved from the beginning - September 1, 1939, when German troops invaded Poland, which was under the rule of the Western Ukraine. This event was preceded by a secret agreement leaders of Nazi Germany and the Soviet Union, according to which western lands were to enter part of the USSR. So on the territory of Western Ukraine was introduced by Soviet troops. German war against the Soviet Union began June 22, 1941 One year later (July 1942) Ukraine was completely captured by German troops. The purpose of the conquerors was the capture Ukraine, extermination of its population, turning it lands on the supplier of food and raw materials. On the territory of Ukraine was established network of death camps. Tens of thousands of people of different nationalities were killed in Kyiv, Kharkiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Rivne.
