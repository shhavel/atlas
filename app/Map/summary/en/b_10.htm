English Revolution of the mid XVII century.

After the death of the childless Queen Elizabeth Tudor dynasty ceased. Scottish throne passed to the house of Stuart. Representatives of the new dynasty (James I and Charles) is also trying to strengthen the absolute power of the monarch, however, makes it likely is not in the interests of the state, and their personal. Therefore, the Parliament and the people to support them. Disputes Stuarts with Parliament went so far that Carl has ceased to convene parliament. But external adventures king needed money collected could provide only the parliament by introducing new taxes. But in exchange for the funds advanced to the king parliament demands were at odds with the absolutist aspirations. August 22, 1642 the king declared war on Parliament. The British divided into supporters of the king and parliament. The country's civil war.

First success was on the side of the king. But soon the army of the Parliament was to win. Contributed to this army of "new model", which was created by Oliver Cromwell. In the decisive battle of Naseby (1645) his "Ironsides" beat the army of the king.

Finally the king was taken prisoner, and at the insistence of Cromwell was executed. England declared a republic.

At the head of the country became Cromwell, who proclaimed Lord Protector. He worried about the economic development of the country, took care of her domination of the seas, led to war with Holland. The Navigation Act of 1651 laid the foundations of sea power of England for the next 300 years. Cromwell began in colonial conquests. The first victims of colonial expansion began to Scotland and Ireland. He thus laid the foundations of the British colonial empire, which later was to become the largest in the world.

In the middle of the country he established a dictatorship based on a rigid Puritan principles. In fact, he held authority over the king.

But after his death, the struggle for power in the country broke out with renewed vigor. In 1660 the monarchy was restored, Stuart returned to the throne (AI Karl Jacob II). But they could not find a common language with the parliament. And then last invited to the English throne of the ruler of Holland, William of Orange. As a result of the "glorious revolution" (1688r.) In England finally established a parliamentary monarchy.

English Revolution of the mid XVII century. was an event of great historical importance. It has established a new economic and socio-political order. In the economic sphere was destroyed everything that hindered the development of market relations and commodity production. After the revolution, the industrial revolution began, in which England has become one of the most developed countries of the world. The invention of the steam engine and the spread of J. Watt subsequently turned England into the "workshop of the world". Development of the industry itself has changed and England. Grew rapidly city arise mines, mills and factories. Once the backward central and northern regions of the country have become the new industrial centers. Industrial supremacy allowed the country to get primacy in trade, to establish control over trade routes and major markets.

In political life, has established a parliamentary monarchy in which the king's power was limited by the Parliament, representing the interests of various strata of English society.

At the beginning of the XVIII century. After merging with the Kingdom of Scotland England turned to the UK. After inclusion of Ireland (1801) state has been called the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland.
