General Information

It lies on the south-east of Ukraine, on the south by the Sea of ​​Azov. Surface - mainly undulating plain (200 m), heavily dissected by river valleys, gullies, ravines. In the north-east - Donetsk Upland, which is west of the region enters the Dnieper Lowland, and in the south-west - the Azov Upland. On limestone and salt deposits are widespread karst landforms abyss career. The main wealth of mineral resources are coal of the Donets Basin, and deposits of rock salt, brown coal, minerals ferrous metallurgy and production of construction materials (dolomite, limestone flux, marl, gypsum, chalk, tripoli, sand, quartzite, granite) . There are reserves for the chemical industry and metallurgy (nefelionovi syenites, fluorite, vermiculite, iron ore - in the Azov Sea, Dawsonite, potassium salt). The world famous Mykytivskomufield mercury. The climate is temperate continental with relatively cold and snowy winters (-7,8 ° in January), hot and dry summers (+ 20,8 °, + 22,8 ° in July). The largest river - the Seversky Donets tributaries (state Butt, Bakhmut). Other rivers: Wolf (Dnieper basin) Kalmius Krynka (Azov Sea). Many rivers dry. 20 reservoirs, ponds and 1011 channel Civerskyy Donets-Donbass somewhat compensate for the deficit of water supply area. Historical Background

First traces of Stone Age man in the region were found in the area Amvrosiyivka. The natural environment contributed to the development of nomadic livestock on these lands. Steppes of Donetsk region for centuries moved numerous nomadic tribes, the Cimmerians, Scythians, Sarmatians, Huns, Avars, Bulgars, Khazars, Magyars, Pechenegs, Cumans. And XIII-XVIII centuries. is dominated by Tatars. The northern part of the region in XVI-XVIII centuries. was a Slobozhanshchina, and its southern part - of the Crimean Khanate and the Cossack Army Lands. In 1734, during the existence of the New Host of the land (near the present city. Artemivska) was incorporated into Samara and Kalmiuskoy palankas. In the XIX century. in the Donetsk region began to build the first mines, steel mills. In the Empire lands were part of Donetsk Kharkiv and Katerynoslavs'ka provinces and to the area of ​​the Don Cossacks. In 1921 there was established Donetsk province in the Soviet Ukraine in the center of town. Bakhmut (Donetsk). City Donetsk appeared in 1869 and by 1924 was called Uzovka (in the 1924-1961 biennium. - Stalin).
