Political map of the world shows the placement of states and dependent territories, showing existing boundaries, capitals and major cities of the planet. In a world of over 240 countries and territories. Of these, more than 200 - sovereign states, ie, political independence of the country. Country - a territory with defined boundaries, populated by certain people, who in the political and geographical terms can have sovereignty (independence), or be a dependent. Around the world there is still more than 40 dependent territories (colonies), each of which is under the political or military control of one of the sovereign states (Gibraltar, New Caledonia, Bermuda, etc.). The proportion of dependent territories in area and population is less than 2%.

Map of "The World in 1914" demonstrates the political picture on the eve of the First World War, while there were only 55 sovereign states and vast colonial empires of France, Britain, Portugal; The Austro-Hungarian, Ottoman, German and Russian Empire.

On the map "The World in 1961" are taken into account political changes after the two world wars and the fall of colonial empires. In the mid-twentieth century. on the site of the former colonies arose over a hundred independent countries in Asia, Africa, Latin America and Oceania.

Comparing these maps, we can observe the continuity of the processes accompanying the history of mankind. With time came and went great civilization arose and destroyed the mighty empire, and once the secondary colonies became influential in the state.
