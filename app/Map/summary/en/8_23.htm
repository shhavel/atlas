PERSONALITY

Philip Orlik (1672-1742) came from an old Czech family. During the Hussite wars Orlik ancestors left the Czech Republic and were in Poland. The future hetman was born October 11, 1672 near Wilno. When he was one year old, his father died. Because raising a son cared for his mother - Ukrainian Orthodox shlyahtyanka. Philip hassle mother received a good education, learning at first in the Jesuit College in Vilnius, and later, when the family moved to Ukraine - Kyiv-Mohyla Academy. With its unusual ability, he has established himself as one of the best students. Orlik quite liked the rhetoric and was a good speaker, wrote poems that were published in the late XVII., Deeply interested in philosophy and literature, fluent in Ukrainian, Polish Church Slavonic, Bulgarian, Serbian, Latin, Italian, German, Swedish, davno- and Modern Greek, probably Turkish. Naturally, joining in 1699 appointed hetman in office, he quickly overcame the service steps and soon (1706) became general secretary. Orlik was initiated in all cases of Hetman Mazepa supported him during the rebellion antymoskovskoho went with him into exile by the end of staying true to the idea of ​​liberation of Ukraine.

ELECTION PYLYP ORLYK Hetman in exile

After the death of Ivan Mazepa and the Cossacks, who went with him into exile, was left without a leader. Faced the need to elect a new Hetman.

Although the candidature of the applicant on the Hetman government almost no one raised objections to the election but because of the unfavorable situation delayed six months.

Finally, April 5, 1710 in Bender (Moldova) took the Cossack Council. Was elected hetman Ivan Mazepa's closest associates - the secretary general of his government of Philip Orlik.

This event started a new chapter in the history of the Ukrainian liberation struggle, because there has never elected hetman abroad. Philip He headed the first powerful Ukrainian political emigration in Western Europe.

Cohesive forces around abroad opposed despotism of Russian autocracy.

APPROVAL "Pacts and Constitutions of Laws and Liberties Cossack Army"

During the Cossack Council of 5 April 1710 was approved document "Pacts and Constitutions of Laws and Freedoms of the Cossack Army" (later the document called the "Constitution of Philip Orlik" and "Bender Constitution").

The basis of "Pacts and Constitutions ..." was an agreement between the hetman and the Cossacks, which acted on behalf of the Ukrainian people, the reciprocal rights and obligations.

This is typical of the traditional document approved hetman's articles were based on agreements between the hetman and the monarch Protector.

For the first time the newly elected Hetman had signed a formal agreement with their constituents, indicating clearly the conditions under which it receives power.

In addition, the paper obґruntovuvavsya political system of Ukraine. That is why it is considered the first Ukrainian Constitution.

Contents of the Constitution of Philip Orlik

The document consists of an introduction and 16 articles.

The main provisions of the articles:

  • proclaimed the independence of Ukraine from Muscovy and the Commonwealth;
  • conditioned by the patronage of the Swedish King and the alliance with the Crimean Khanate;
  • territory of Ukraine is determined in accordance with the contract Zborowski 1649 .;
  • Cossacks returned to their traditional territory in the Dnieper;
  • hetman formed the General Council of the legislature, consisting of general officers, colonels, elected deputies from each regiment and the delegates of the Cossacks;
  • Council met three times a year - at Christmas, Easter, Intercession;
  • cases of injustice and guilt Hetman elders saw General Court, to which the hetman had no right to interfere;
  • treasury and property were subject to the general pidskarbiyu for the maintenance of certain land intended Hetman;
  • mounted colonels, captains of their subsequent approval by Hetman;
  • special commission was to carry out the audit of public lands utilized by the officers, and duties of the population; Cossacks hetman was to protect all people from excessive taxes and duties, Cossack help widows and orphans.

Ukraine proclaimed an independent republic, the Constitution of Philip Orlik was the highest level of contemporary political thought, not only in Ukraine, but also in general in Europe, because no country at that time had no such documents.

Constitution greatly curtailed the rights of the hetman, included the establishment of a representative body - the General Council. It laid the foundation for the principle of separation of powers into executive and judicial branches, implemented elected positions. These features document shrewdly anticipated future directions of development of democratic states.

Hike Orlik Right Bank Ukraine in 1711

During the year Orlik managed to create anti-Moscow coalition of Sweden and the Crimean Khanate, Turkey, the government Orlik, as well as those of the Polish forces that supported Stanislaw Leszczynski. Allies had a plan military campaigns on the Right Bank Ukraine.

January 31, 1711 detachments of Cossacks led by Kostya Gordienko under the leadership of Philip Orlik went from Bender.

Along the way they were joined by Polish and Tatar troops. Allied troops quickly proceeded to the Right Bank.

Ukrainian population of joy greeted Orlik, passed without a fight town. The Right Bank shelves, except Bilotserkivskyi acknowledged Orlik for Hetman and went to his side.

Statements against Moscow administration began in Left-Bank Ukraine.

Left Bank Hetman Ivan Skoropadskyi sent against Orlik shelves, but they were defeated.

In late March, the troops came under Orlik White Church, the strongest fortress on the right bank, and surrounded her, but soon due to the lack of heavy guns needed for the attack, were forced to retreat.

Determined the fate of the campaign and the approach of the Moscow army and treacherous action has long been uncertain Tatar allies. In late April, Orlik and Cossacks had to return to Bandar.

According to the plan of joint actions against Moscow in late January 1711 with Khan 50,000th horde left the Crimea and moved along the lower Dnieper, and then returned to Kharkiv.

Almost meeting no resistance, Tatars captured Slobozhanshchina However, going to Kharkov, Horde suddenly retreated and returned to Crimea.

Instead of liberation Horde campaign has once again brought to the Ukrainian land robbery, fire; thousands of Ukrainian captured.

Prut campaign of Peter I

In the spring of 1711 began the Moscow counteroffensive. Tsar Peter I declared war on Turkey.

Moscow army went through Ukrainian territory to Moldavia.

Entered the territory of the Right-Bank Ukraine, the king's army on the orders of Peter the destroyed towns and villages, tortured the local people, tens of thousands were driven in Left Bank Ukraine.

In June 1711 the main forces of Moscow's troops that they had led Peter, together with detachments ally - Moldavian master - came to Iasi.

In early July, came to the area Falchi Turkish-Tatar army. On July 8-9, the decisive battle.

Army of Peter I was surrounded, she threatened the entire lesion.

But Moscow Command settled case: faint Turkish army chief was a large bribe and agreed to negotiate.

Prut Peace Treaty in 1711 and its implications for Ukraine

Negotiations between Muscovy and Turkey resulted in the conclusion of a peace treaty. It was signed on 12 July 1711 at pp. Rod near Iasi.

By agreement of the Moscow government undertook to convey Turkey of Ukrainian lands, including the Azov with the surrounding area.

Moscow promised to destroy their newly built fortress - Taganrog, Stone backwater on the Dnieper, Novobohorodytsku fortress in the estuary. Samara (now within Dnipropetrovsk).

In addition, the Moscow government has the obligation not to interfere in Polish affairs, denied Zaporozhye and Right-Bank Ukraine.

As for the Left Bank, the article about it was formulated so ambiguous that each side could interpret it differently.

Over the Prut Peace Treaty of Turkey in Moscow to allow the army to withdraw its troops from the artillery and wagon train from Moldova.

Prut Peace was not profitable for Moscow. Having emerged from the entourage, Peter violated the terms of the contract.

It made Orlik take steps to persuade the Turkish government not to approve contractual documents as long as Moscow does not fulfill all obligations and do not give up all of Ukraine. However, the Turks under the pressure of Britain and the Netherlands have ratified the treaty.

However, when ratifying the Prut Peace Treaty in Istanbul Turkish government forced Peter the urgent obligation to fulfill Azov and Taganrog, and begin the withdrawal of troops from Poland and those land-bank Ukraine, which belonged to Poland under the contract in 1686

March 5, 1712 issued a firman (decree) of Sultan, followed by the Right-Bank Ukraine and Sich distributed power Orlik.

Next month, Turkey signed an agreement with Moscow: Left bank of Kyiv and its suburbs remained for Muscovy.

Ratification of the treaty Prut in this form became heavy blow to Orlik and his associates. After all, Ukraine has remained divided over the Left Bank continued to dominate Moscow.

Authorities are on the right-bank Ukraine Orlik received by the sultan's firman, because of the intransigence of Poland was too elusive, two years later, in April 1714, between Turkey and Poland have signed an agreement under which the right-bank Ukraine remained in Poland.

So hope Philip Orlik the rapid liberation of Ukraine failed. He himself remained Hetman in exile, because no patronage of the Swedish king, no alliance with Crimea, or the recognition of the owner of the Right-Bank Ukraine from Turkey or Host support troops not vtoruvaly his way to Ukraine.

ATTEMPTS TO CREATE ORLYK coalition antymoskovskoyi

In June 1714 Orlik had to leave the bender to Western Europe.

At the invitation of Karl XII and Philip Orlik part of the officers moved to Sweden.

In 1720 he moved to Germany and later to France.

From 1722 to move to Turkey, where he spent the last 20 years.

And no matter where the Orlik, he never once forgot about the main goal of the activity, which saw the creation antymoskovskoyi coalition. Hetman believed that only with the help of other countries can liberate Ukraine from the yoke of empire and unite all Ukrainian lands into a strong state.

Seeking the support of European countries such as England, France, the German states, Poland, Sweden, he sent many letters zverennya governments, breaking them Ukrainian question.

Philip Orlik lost no opportunity to touch the subject of Ukraine, or in direct contact with Charles XII, King Louis XIV, Augustus II, Stanislaw Leszczynski, Sultan and khans, or meeting with key ministers most influential states.
