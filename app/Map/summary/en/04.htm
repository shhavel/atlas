The population of Ukraine is 46 372.7 ths. Persons (01.01.2008.). By this measure, our country is the sixth largest country in Europe after Russia, Germany, Britain, Italy and France.

The average population density of Ukraine - 77 persons per 1 km2, but placed it evenly. Placement of the population with a map shows that the most common density - in industrialized Donetsk region and surrounding areas, as well as in Galicia, Bukovina and Transcarpathia. The smallest - in Chernihiv region, as well as in Polesie and the steppe zone. The greatest density of rural population in the western regions due to historical factors - these lands were inhabited and mastered much earlier than the south and east of Ukraine.

Population Dynamics Ukraine reflects the impact of the Cossack era of permanent war (Poland, Turkey, Russia), the hard times of the Soviet era (1917-20's liberation movement., Famines 1920-22 biennium., 1932-33., Mass repression 1937-38 biennium ., World War II, post-war devastation). To some extent, the number of people affected by the Soviet government stimulated out-migration to the eastern regions of the USSR in 1953-54's. The Chornobyl disaster and 1986.

The demographic crisis, the preconditions which have developed during the Soviet era, fully manifested itself in 1993-1994. Although, thanks to the improved economic situation and the implementation of government measures to stimulate the birth rate, demographic trends slightly decreased, however, depopulation and an aging population in the state continues.

Long-term demographic crisis led to population decline Ukraine especially in rural areas, moreover, is now in a state not five, but four cities-millionaires (in once millionth Donetsk is now home to 988 thousand people)

Donetsk, Kharkiv, Luhansk, Dnipropetrovsk region is the most urbanized in Ukraine. It is in this region is concentrated the largest number of state industrial enterprises. This is due to the fact that in the postwar years, it is in these areas was directed main stream of people from other Soviet republics and regions of Ukraine to rebuild the state's economy.

The predominance of women in Ukraine of the population due to the fact that men are more often employed in sectors of the economy associated with increased injuries and life-threatening. In addition, it is the male foundation of any army, therefore, the greatest loss of life of the country in the war falls on them.
