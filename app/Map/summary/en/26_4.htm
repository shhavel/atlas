"Cars - in every family", this slogan is a reality in Ukraine. For many of our fellow citizens no longer a luxury car and a normal vehicle. Today, the average Ukrainian can purchase a car, depending on their preferences and wealth. The number of "iron horses" in Ukraine has been growing steadily in Kiev, for example, in 2008 there were already nearly 1 million cars, many of them in the Dnipropetrovsk, Donetsk regions. As the number of cars per 1000 inhabitants lead Kyiv and Zaporizhia (the main center of the country's automotive industry).

However, in addition to the convenience and comfort of a solid motorization causes and negative effects. In big cities, cars are the main sources of air pollution, traffic jams form, complicating the operation of public transport and so on. These circumstances have forced state and local governments to direct significant resources to improving road network and all transport infrastructure.
