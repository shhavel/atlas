The abolition of serfdom in the Ukrainian lands

The new capitalist relations, which originated in the late eighteenth - early nineteenth century. persistently demanded abolition of serfdom, which was an obstacle to further economic development of Ukrainian lands.

Further elaborated these contradictions Crimean War (1853-1856 gg.), Which Russia lost.

January 3, 1857 was created the Secret Committee, later renamed the Home Committee on Peasant Affairs. Peasant question had to decide nobles.

February 19, 1861, Tsar Alexander II issued a manifesto on the abolition of serfdom and the "Terms of peasants liberated from bondage." According to these documents peasants became personally free, but the landlords left the ownership of the land.

During the agrarian reform Ukraine's territory was divided into regions according to the specifics of event:

1) communal land tenure (province Novorossiysk Territory);

2) homestead land (Left-bank Ukraine);

Reform has been saved large landholdings.

Farmers received economic rights (to buy property, engage in commerce and crafts, make factories).

However, the peasantry remained lower classes in the country with restricted movement.

As a result of land reform farmers in the south and east, lost 30% of its holdings. On the Right Bank government increased peasant holdings by 20%, in order to attract farmers to his side and weaken the Polish nobility.

Within 49 years the peasants had to pay indemnity payments. On the Right Bank redemption fee reduced by 20%. Implemented a system of peasant management: rural communities united in the parish is established solidarity for the payment of taxes.

The consequences of reform:

1) there have been fundamental changes in the distribution of land ownership;

2) commodity-money relations became dominant in the farms of landlords and rich peasants;

3) becomes clearer specialization of certain regions of Ukraine;

4) Different methods of land use (rent, maintenance of the farm);

5) increased the yield of p / crops from the use of machines, hired labor, improving the structure of crops.

In 1864 was held rural reform. Created a system of local government. On the Left Bank Ukraine created 6 provincial and 60 district zemstvo. Right Bank Ukraine zemstvo government was established in 1911 The self-government in rural council, the provincial zemstvo assembly. Executive departments - provincial and district zemstvo board. Elections were held for property qualifications for three years.

Zemstva involved in the organization of health care, development of education, mail, collect statistics uporyadkovuvaly road.

In 1864 was made the judiciary. Unlike birth and closed the court entered pozastanovyy open, independent court. Introduced the jury judge. The trial took place with the participation of two parties: the defense and prosecution. In Ukraine, was established three chambers: Kyiv, Kharkiv and Odessa. Judgment rendered without a jury, could be challenged in court chambers. Were instituted magistrates were solving small business. Cassation functions performed Senate.

In 1864 began to reform education. According to the "Regulations on primary public schools" introduced a unified system of primary education. In the field of secondary education created a classic men's and women's gymnasium. The tuition was very high. The right to enter university graduates had a classical schools. Graduates of women's rights at school entry had.

In 1865 made reform of censorship. There were special bodies censorship.

In the 1870 municipal reform done. All cities in Ukraine created municipal councils. The election was based on property qualification. Executive authority council - city council, headed by the chairman. City Government in charge of the city.

There was also military reform (1864-1883 gg.). Ukraine has established three military districts: Kyiv, Odesa, Kharkiv. The reform has significantly strengthened the army. Started to operate a new military regulations. Introduced universal conscription (lifetime of 6 years in the Navy - 7). Clergy exempt from service. Opened military schools and grammar schools, cadet schools. Held rearmament.

Financial reform (1860-1864 gg.). A state bank, put a single Government Accountability Center, excise taxation of alcoholic beverages increased taxes on consumer goods, created a unified state fund that concentrated in the hands of all income and expenses of the state.

Reforms 60-70 years were limited, inconsistent and half-hearted, but still they have created conditions for economic and political development in the new environment. Developing industry, trade, growing cities and urban population, rural population engaged in industry. Opened the way to becoming an industrial society.
