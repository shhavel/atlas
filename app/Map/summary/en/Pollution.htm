The major air pollutants in large cities is motor vehicles, power plants and industrial plants.

Compared with cities like Donetsk, Krivoy Rog, Dnipropetrovsk in Kiev smaller companies that heavily polluting. But in our city this problem is very serious.

CHP supplying heat in our homes, burning with coal, gas and oil. Products of combustion are emitted into the atmosphere and causing serious damage to the environment and human health.

Cars running on gasoline and solar oil and also throwing with exhaust fumes large number of hazardous substances. Given that in our town of 500 thousand cars can only imagine what damage they cause to the environment.

To reduce emissions by various actions. Industrial enterprises introduced advanced clean technologies installed treatment plants. A particularly bad company submitted outside the city also created around them sanitary zones (areas where housing is prohibited and localization of social purposes).

With regard to transport most effective measures is to limit traffic, strict adherence to the requirements for owners of cars environmentally friendly engines. Also of great importance is the condition of roads, provision of transportation, fuel quality, the amount of green space in the city and more.
