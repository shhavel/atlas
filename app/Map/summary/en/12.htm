Mastering Peninsula man begins in the late Acheulian 400-100 thousand. Years ago. Habitable area was basically Internal ridge where the famous park, located under the rock shelter. In open terrain parking appear mainly in the Mesolithic-Neolithic.

In the Eneolithic-Bronze Age (III - II millennium BC.) In the plains inhabited by agricultural and pastoral tribes, whose monuments are numerous settlements and burial mounds, the latter used for the subsequent intake of burial tribes until the late Middle Ages.

At the beginning of the I millennium BC. in connection with the formation of steppe landscapes are settled nomads.

The Crimean peninsula since ancient times differed noticeable dynamics of ethnic processes, that was largely due to its geographical position. Natural contrasts - a combination of steppe and mountain landscapes, organic connection with the Eurasian steppes to the north and marine environment - contributed to the influx of nomadic populations and influence from the civilizations of the Mediterranean basin. It was back to the early maritime contacts are the first information in the written sources of ethnic communities inhabiting the peninsula.

There is a possibility of identifying archaeological complexes with native ethnonyms mentioned by ancient authors. Often this involves serious difficulties and generates heated debate, often continuing for decades.

The earliest historical nomadic tribes known to us under the name of the Cimmerians. In VIII - VII century. BC they were replaced by the Scythians, dominated the Crimea right up to the beginning of our era.
