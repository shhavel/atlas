The situation with respect to Ukraine time zones displayed on the map Timezones world. Ukraine is the second (CEE) time zone, ie noon in the country comes to 2 hours earlier than zero (Western European) time zone.

Since the length of the territory in terms of degree - 18 ° 05 '(with a width of one time zone 15 °), Ukraine could be in three time zones, but for ease of farming it is assumed that the entire territory of the country is in one time zone. This practice is common in other countries.

For a fuller use of the daylight portion of the day during the year introduced daylight saving time, from the last Sunday in March to last Sunday in October clocks are moved 1 hour forward. So in the summer we are ahead of standard time by 1 hour.
