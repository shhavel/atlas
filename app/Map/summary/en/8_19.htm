PERSONALITY

Ivan Sirko was born, probably in the early XVII century. Vinnichchine in a family of small Ukrainian nobleman. His life and work of the period before Chortomlyk Host remained little information. With the future Ukrainian Hetman Bohdan Khmelnytsky even in 1646 was in France, where the Cossacks fought with the army of the Prince of Conde. Historical documents indicate the part of Ivan Sirko in the National War, particularly in the siege Zhvanetskiy autumn 1653 at least 15 times he was elected ataman in Zaporozhye. He was a prominent military leader who spent more than 60 battles against the forces of the Ottoman Empire, the Crimean Khanate and the Nogai hordes and never defeated. Corresponded with the hetman of Ukraine, the monarchs of Muscovy, the Commonwealth, the Crimea and Turkey. He wrote about the then European newspapers, the writers and chroniclers of many countries, it sang Ukrainian and foreign poets. Lassie - the hero of many folk legends.

Sich played a leading role in the preparation and deployment of the National Liberation War of the Ukrainian people against Polish domination.

Experience in public life, which for centuries nahromadzhuvavsya in Zaporozhye was creatively used Bohdan Khmelnytsky.

In the liberated territories implemented Cossack system. The Cossacks took part in military operations against the Commonwealth.

However, under the terms of the Treaty of Zboriv 1649 many Cossacks found themselves out of the Cossack Registry.

This caused discontent Sich and exacerbation of their relations with the Hetman's power.

Not gone round the Cossacks and difficult for Ukraine Bilotserkivskiy contract. It is no accident Sich participated in presentations to officers and Cossacks hetman.

Strained relations Zaporozhian Sich with the Hetman's government led to a change in the location.

In 1652 the Cossacks moved their capital from the open plain in front Mikitin Horn near the Dnieper wetlands near the mouth. Chortomlyk.

ZaporizhzhyaJanuary implicit in the estuary. Chortomlyk, has existed for years 1652-1709. In the historical literature it was called Chortomlyk.

However, to continue the relationship with the Hetman of the Zaporozhian Sich authorities consist uneasy, sometimes magnified up to armed intervention.

Most clearly this proved to be the death of Bohdan Khmelnytsky, when increased interference in other countries internal affairs of Ukraine, a struggle for the hetman's mace and began to strengthen the power of the ruler, which is often neglected of Cossack masses.

The policy of the Moscow government in Zaporizhzhya Sich

The imperial government always treated to Sich biased, seeing in it an ominous fire dissent and freedom.

That is why Moscow has sought to conquer in January to make the most of its military and economic capabilities.

The first event in the implementation of insidious intent was hosted at Host Moscow garrison, which appeared in Chortomlyk Host 1663

The reason for its location was declared the joint struggle against the Turkish-Tatar invaders.

Muscovites were instructed to work together with the Cossacks against the Crimean and Nogai hordes.

In garrison was assigned as the organization of intelligence: gathering information about the internal situation of the Crimean Khanate and the Nogai hordes of their foreign policy plans and the events that took place in Poland, Turkey and others.

However, in addition to the published orders of the tsarist government, there were also secret instructions.

According to them:

  • commander of the garrison was obliged to follow all the events that took place in Zaporozhye;
  • had to report to Moscow on relations with the hetman of the Zaporozhian Sich, individual officers Hetmanate groups;
  • report all diplomatic relations Sich.

Under the terms of the truce Andrusovo dual Polish-Muscovite control of Zaporozhye 1667 royal garrisons were withdrawn from Host.

It did not put an end to interference in the internal affairs of tsarist Host.

After a gradual restriction of civil rights in Ukraine, the desire of the tsarist government to extend its authority to all Ukrainian lands could not affect the Sich - the core of Ukrainian statehood.
