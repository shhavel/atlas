Despite the princely feuds Kievan Rus met 13th century. rich and prosperous country. However, from the East threatened new enemy - the Mongols, who in the Asian steppes formed a powerful state and started to conquer neighboring nations.

The first hike of the Mongol hordes to the Russian lands held in 1222-1223's. At that time, there were no significant damage. However, these hikes, headed by Batu Khan, had for the Russian lands serious consequences. The greatest devastation suffered Kiev, Chernigov and Pereyaslav land. In the conquered territories of the Urals and Western Siberia to the Black Sea was established Mongolian state - the Golden Horde. Under its authority was and Rus-Ukraine.
