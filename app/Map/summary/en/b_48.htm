Japan is a highly developed post-industrial country that occupies a leading position in the world economic power and size of GDP per capita.

Japan has favorable economic and geographical position in the center of the Asia-Pacific region, which has become one of the major economic centers of the world.

In Japan, developed all areas of the modern economy. It combines high-intensity production based on high-tech and non-productive sphere, targeted to high standards of living. The industry achieved high levels of automation and robotics cybernation production, introduction of energy saving technologies. An important feature of the Japanese economy is the coexistence of many small businesses with giant corporations. Six major industrial and financial groups control almost all aspects of economic life.

Territorial structure in the country is allocated area of ​​high concentration of industry on the Pacific coast (Pacific industrial belt) and peripheral zone - the Sea of ​​Japan. Limited resources of its own raw materials led to a concentration of industrial production in the areas closest to the import of raw materials (east coast ports of Tokyo, Yokohama, Osaka and Kobe). It is here formed the main industrial zone of the country, where about 90% of industrial output. In the Pacific industrial belt accounts for about 70% of the population. Metropolitan areas of the Pacific Coast (Tokyo - Yokohama, Osaka - Kobe - Kyoto, Nagoya) and located between the cities form a single system of urban settlements - Tokyo megalopolis (Tokaido), which is the largest in the world. There are about 60 million people.

The role of agriculture in the economy is low. It gives less than three percent of GDP of the country and provides only 70% of the needs of the population for food. Japan - the world's largest importer of agricultural products. The main area of ​​specialization of agriculture, rice cultivation, gardening and fishing. In the north-east and the Sea of ​​Japan extends the so-called rice belt. Japan is fishing in all ocean areas. The country is the world leader in fishing and seafood.

In MHPP Japan is one of the world's financial centers (15% of foreign direct investment in the world), and the largest manufacturer of high-tech high-tech industries (Sony, Panasonic, Toshiba). Country - the world's largest car manufacturer (Toyota, Lexus, Honda, Nissan, Subaru).
