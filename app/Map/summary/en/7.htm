His reign was a time of consolidation and strengthening the authority of the Grand Unity of. Unity state retained and son Mstislav Monomakh. However, after his death began the era of fragmentation - the so-called period when Russia broke up into separate kingdoms, which would not obey Kyiv, and sought to live an independent life.
