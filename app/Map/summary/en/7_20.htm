1. Development of the Ukrainian language

Ukrainian origins date back to ancient Slavic people, still peredderzhavnyy times.

Then, according to scientists, began to form the Ukrainian language, because the formation of a separate nation and its language development occurs simultaneously.

In the 9-13 centuries. in our language already had most of the features that distinguish it from other Slavic languages.

It also differed from the modern. That is why the time history of language researchers use the term pre-Ukrainian.

Writing

Language researchers say that in the second half of the 9th - early 10th century. our ancestors there was writing.

That ancient alphabet was created from the Greek - and this is not surprising given the long association of the Eastern Slavs with the Greeks; Byzantium with which the young Russian state had the closest relationship, the official language was also Greek.

Evidence of their own original alphabet in old Ukrainian is finding inscriptions on the walls of St. Sophia Cathedral.

Kyiv scribe in the 11th century. carefully wrote on the walls just built the temple of the alphabet that differs from prevailing at the time the Cyrillic created for the transfer in writing in one of the South Slavic languages ​​using Greek letters brothers Cyril and Methodius and transferred to our territories with the adoption of Christianity.

Significantly different from narodnorozmovnoyi language, she still had it in common, as based on close, too Slavic language, and so was understandable, especially educated people - clergy, princes and boyars.

Scientists say that your Kyiv Rus existed two options written language, Church Slavonic, which were written the most important liturgical books, and Church Slavonic with large patches of live speech (for a later time it is called Old Ukrainian portrait) - her writing various documents and literary works secular trend.
