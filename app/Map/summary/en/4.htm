General Information

It lies in the south-eastern part of Ukraine, in the basin of the middle and lower reaches of the Dnieper. Surface area - rolling plain (100-200 m). On the right bank of the Dnieper Plateau foothills (192 m) decreasing from north-west to south-east to the valley of the Dnieper and break it steep scarp, and in the south - go into the Black Sea Lowland. Left Bank adheres to the Dnieper Lowland. The south-east - spurs Azov Upland. The surface of a dissected by deep river valleys, gullies, ravines. Scope - one of the richest in Ukraine by the diversity and volume of mineral resources: Rog Iron and Nikopol manganese ore basins, Western Donbass (coal), brown coal of the Dnieper basin, oil and natural gas deposits of titanium ore, bauxite, nickel, cobalt, granite, basalt, marble, kaolin, dolomite, refractory clay roofing slates, minium, limestone, sand and more. The climate is continental with hot and dry summers (+ 21,5 °, + 22,5 ° in July), with frequent downpours, strong winds, causing drought; with mild, dry winters (-4,5 °, -6,5 ° in January) with frequent thaws and ice. Precipitation - 400 - 490 mm per year, mainly in the warm period. In the 55 rivers longer than 25 km. The main water artery - the Dnieper. The left tributaries, Orel, Samara with wolf, right - Bazavluk, Wet Sura, Ingulets of Saksahannyu. There are three large (Dnieper) and 100 small reservoirs, ponds 1400. Lakes are few and small. Canals Dnieper-Krivoy Rog and Dnieper-Donets Basin. Soil: ordinary and southern chernozems (48% of the area), meadow-chernozem, meadow saline, turf, sand and more. Natural vegetation riznotravnotypchakovo-grass, watersheds - shrub; ravine forest (elm, oak, pear, ash, linden, pine) and floodplain (oak, elm, linden, ash, elm, elm, maple, alder, etc.). Historical Background

The first traces of "pralyudyny" found here are, as scientists believe, before the Early Stone Age. Scientists say that it is on the Dnieper 6 thousand. Years ago were first domesticated the horse. In the mounds in the area found Novomoskovsky four-wheeled carts, one of the oldest in Eastern Europe. Among the findings of archaeologists - the world-famous treasures of the ancient Scythians - Pectoral with thick Graves and crest of the mound Soloha.U Middle Ages these lands held northern boundary domains nomads. Dnieper ran the famous trade route "from the Varangians to the Greeks". In 972 g. Prince Svyatoslav here, near the Dnieper cataracts, was killed in battle with the Pechenegs. Dnipropetrovsk region known as the land of the Cossack Sich - five of them existed in modern Nikopol district. Inclusion Zaporozhian lands of the Russian Empire and the elimination of the Sich in 1775 accompanied by the formation in southern Ukraine Novorossiysk first, and then Katerynoslavs'ka provinces of the main city Yekaterynoslav (Dnipropetrovsk). Large changes in the life of the region associated with the opening end of the XIX century. significant mineral deposits - coal, iron and manganese ores. At this time Katerynoslav province is fast becoming a center of industry south of the Russian Empire. Put into operation in 1884 Catherine railway uniting and Donetsk coal Krivoy Rog Mining pools.
