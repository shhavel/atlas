Central Botanical Garden. Mikhail Grishko wascreated in 1935. For the pre-war years, there were gathered a large collection of plants, but during the Nazi occupation of the plants were destroyed, and the garden has suffered considerable damage. Renaissance Botanical Garden began after the liberation of Kyiv in 1944, and to visit it was discovered only in 1964.

Today, the 132-hectare garden contains a unique collection of 12,000 species, forms and varieties of plants from all over the world. Every spring thousands of visitors attracts the largest collection of lilacs in Ukraine. In greenhouses garden a unique collection of tropical and subtropical plants, among which one of the largest - collection of orchids. In 1980 orchids from the collections of the Botanical Garden first visited in space aboard the space station & laquo; & raquo ;. Salute

In the garden created botanical and geographical areas of the Carpathians, Ukraine Steppes, Woodlands, Crimea, Caucasus, Altai and Western Siberia, Central Asia, the Far East.

In the botanical garden made scientific research on acclimatization of plants, the gene pool conservation and plant breeding. Besides the works on gardening and landscaping industries, residential areas, houses, and a large environmental, educational work of ecological philosophy of man.
