Environmental problems - the problems caused by human interaction with the environment.

In the second polovnyk twentieth century. due to excessive human impact on nature in many countries and regions of the world environmental situation reached the level of environmental crisis. Ecological crisis - a stress state between man and nature as much human nature begins to change dramatically negative impact on social development, and endanger the existence of the human species. This is the opposite condition in which humanity is subject to timely respond adequately can still remedy the situation.

The world, unfortunately, is a territory whose state can be described as an ecological catastrophe - an abnormal state of nature, arising as a result of human activity, and leads to extremely negative consequences, and increased morbidity and mortality. An example of local ecological disaster zone is the Chernobyl accident (Ukraine, Belarus, Russia) and the Ural region (Russia), where in the last decades of the twentieth century. resulting from depletion and pollution of water and air dramatically worsened the living conditions of the population.
