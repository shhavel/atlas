Eighty Years' War

Netherlands (in translation - "reduce the earth") is called the land located on the lower Rhine, Meuse, Scheldt and the North Sea coast. In this small area there were about 300 cities. Due to the great geographical discoveries Netherlands were at the crossroads of new trade routes, and the city of Antwerp became the center of world trade. It is on the Antwerp exchange rates were determined on the eastern and European goods.

Since the beginning of the XVI century. Netherlands were part of the Holy Roman Empire of the German Nation, simultaneously entering into possession of the Spanish royal family of Habsburg. During the reign of the Spanish King Philip II (1556-1598 AD), 17 provinces of the Netherlands, which included industrialized related trade links the city enveloped discontent. He was summoned by Philip II attempts to establish order here, like Spanish: complete economic and political dependence on the king.

1566 a delegation of Dutch nobles turned to the Spanish king namestnitsa Margaret of Parma to cease operations in the country Inquisition persecuted Calvinists, and permit the convening of the States-General. However, this request was not granted.

In August 1566 in the Netherlands uprising began iconoclasts. It covered 12 of the 17 provinces. At this time the country was destroyed about 5,500 Catholic churches and monasteries - the support of the Spanish government. Destroyed a number of masterpieces of religious art. In fact, the revolution broke out in the country, as a response to the policy of the Spanish authorities. On the orders of Philip II in the Netherlands entered the Spanish troops led by the Duke of Alba - the new governor of the country.

During the period of the provincial government of the Duke of Alba (1567-1573 biennium) in the country of bloody terror reigned. It killed about 11 thousand. People.

The country turned guerrilla struggle against the Spaniards. The impetus for a general uprising and liberation of the northern coast of the Netherlands was the capture of the city by sea gezam Bril. To lead the uprising was invited Prince William of Orange, who previously headed the Dutch nobility, dissatisfied by the Spanish authorities.

The Duke of Alba tried to return to obedience rebellious province by force. But all his efforts were in vain because of the heroic resistance of the local people.

Catholic nobility of the Southern Netherlands, funky deployment rebellion went on reconciliation with the Spaniards. January 6, 1579 in the town of Arras was signed by the union which they recognized the sovereignty of Philip II and Catholicism as the religion of a single authorization.

Northern Province in response to that concluded Union of Utrecht, which included a joint prosecution of the war against Spain to complete victory, creating a unified army, the monetary system and a common foreign policy. Signing of the Union of Utrecht beginning emergence of the new state - the Republic of the United Provinces, it is often called Holland. AI Philip William of Orange announced a major rebel and a reward for his murder. In response, the 26 July 1581 the States General of the Netherlands took the act of independence from Spain.

Spain finally recognized the independence of the United Provinces only in 1648 she became one of the most powerful European states XVII. Under the rule of the Spanish Habsburgs were the Southern Netherlands (modern Belgium).
