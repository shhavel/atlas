We are citizens of Ukraine - a large European country. Among more than 200 countries, our country has a special place because of its considerable economic, scientific and human potential.

The area of ​​Ukraine 603 700 square meters. km. For this indicator, it ranks 41 in the world and 2 in Europe. Our country stretches 1,316 km from west to east and 893 km from north to south. The length of its borders is 6500 km.

Ukraine borders with seven countries. Our northern neighbor is Belarus, north-east - Russia. In western Ukraine borders with Poland, Slovakia and Hungary. To the southwest of our country is the border with Romania and Moldova. From the south Ukraine is washed by the Black and Azov Seas.

Ukraine is a unitary state. Administratively, it consists of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea, 24 oblasts and two cities with special status (Kyiv and Sevastopol).
