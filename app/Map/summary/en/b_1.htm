Ukraine - a European country not only by geographic location but also by historical, ethnic, cultural and spiritual roots. It is - part of the common European space that extends from the Iberian Peninsula to the Urals, from Scandinavia to Sicily.

Sovereign Ukraine joined the family of European nations after independence in 1991

The area of ​​our country 603.7 thousand km sq., Which is 5.7% of Europe. The total length of 6500 km of borders.

Geographical location of Ukraine determined by the location of its territory in the south of the East European Plain (between 22 ° and 40 ° east longitude and 45 ° and 52 ° north latitude) in central Europe. The vast territory of plainness, mild temperate continental climate, fertile soils are favorable for habitation and economic activity of the population.

Ukraine is bordered by Belarus, Russia, Moldova, Romania, Hungary, Slovakia and Poland. All of our neighbors - the state transition that are either already members of the European Union or seek to work closely with this authoritative international organization, which guarantees stability and prosperity in Europe.

The geographical position of Ukraine is very favorable. Pass through its territory transport routes connecting Asia, Western and Central Europe. These include 10 international railways, highways, pipelines. Ukraine has 19 ports, there are ferry (Illichivs'k - Varna Illichivs'k - Poti). Through Danube carried connection with many countries of the continent.
