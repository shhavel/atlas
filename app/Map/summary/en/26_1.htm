The main purpose of health facilities - disease prevention and treatment of people. Although the level of physicians in Ukraine - one of the highest in the world, the poor financial situation of the institutions, the slow implementation of health insurance, the general low standard of living adversely affect the condition of affairs in the industry.

Today, the best equipped health institutions and medical personnel Kyiv, Dnipropetrovsk, Kharkiv, Luhansk, Lviv, Chernihiv region. In Volyn, Zhytomyr, Transcarpathian regions corresponding figures are twice lower. An important component of the social sector of Ukraine are domestic services. The ability to timely and accurately repair home appliances, car or make a good hairstyle at the hairdresser are also better equipped with residents of the capital and major cities. Residents of villages and small towns are just beginning to get used to the benefits of civilization. Significant development of recent years have become objects of retail and restaurant facilities. At the time of the planned economy, this sector has been very poorly developed, so now trade and restaurant business is the most profitable and therefore here directed largest investment flows.

Given that the profitability of trading and restaurant business is highly dependent on the availability of effective consumer, of course, that these objects are most widely used in major cities, tourist and recreation areas. However, retail outlets and catering is available today in almost every village.
