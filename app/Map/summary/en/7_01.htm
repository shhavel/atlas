Men first appeared in Ukraine about 1 million years ago. The oldest monument in Ukraine there is parking near the village of Queen Vinogradov District in Transcarpathia.

On the left bank of the Tisa, on the terrace height of 100-120 meters, archaeologists have discovered a unique archaeological finds, the many stone tools - are covered with pebbles, hand axes, hostrokonechnyky, scrapers, edge Rohatyn, knives.

Bone ancient inhabitants of Ukraine so far reveal no luck. But scientists believe that it was archanthropines. They came to the territory of Europe from Africa at the beginning of glaciation. The climate was still mild, but already felt the cold breath of the glacier.

For archanthropinae was characterized by:

  • The use of fire, but they could not produce it.
  • Tools were made of stone shaking.
  • Main tools - hand axes, which could push, cut, cut.
  • In stone, used wooden tools, such as spears, pointed the way to the end of the burning fire.
  • They lived in large groups - herds. They do not build homes, constantly moving in search of food. In those times in our area have had a lot of heat-loving plants. So the ancient inhabitants of the Ukrainian lands nourished by their fruits, shoots, roots. Eaten as meat, hunting for deer, antelope, horses, bulls, elephants, rhinos, hippos.

Traces activity archanthropinae found near the village Rokosovo in Transcarpathia, near the villages of Luka Vrublovetska and Babin on the Dniester and Amvrosiyivka in the Donbass, in the foothills of the Crimea and more.

Changes in environmental conditions

Due to icing posuvorishav climate: observed sharp contrasts in temperature (hot summers and bitter winters), and rainfall was the same as in modern semi.

Glacier advanced to the northern part of Ukraine, and along the Dnieper reached modern Dnipropetrovsk. Since it constantly blowing strong cold and dry winds.

Areas immediately adjacent to the glacier turned into a cold desert. To the south up to the Carpathians and the Black Sea stretches of tundra and cold steppes.

Under these conditions, heat-loving plants and animals disappeared. Instead, there were northern. The animals, for example, in Ukraine lived reindeer, arctic foxes, mammoths, hairy rhinoceros, musk ox and more.

Adapting to the harsh climate, the people of those times often settled in caves. This is evidenced by the numerous finds traces of Neanderthals found in caves in the Crimea.

In areas where there were caves, Neanderthals built homes. They have a rounded shape. Base housing was taught from mammoth bones or stones. For walls usually branches that stuck between the bones, and the top housing nakryvalysya skins of animals.

At that time people began to use clothing.

Mastered the ability to make fire.

Tools made using blanks in the form of flakes or plates.

The most common tools Neanderthals were hostrokonechnyky and scrapers. Hostrokonechnyky ruled by the edge to spears, scrapers - for knives.

With Neanderthals, scientists have linked the first stirrings of the spiritual life.

By Neanderthals belonged to the oldest person in the territory of Ukraine, whose existence is attested by the discovery of not only homes or parking lots, and remains of the man himself.

The cave Kyyik Koba, Crimea, was excavated Neanderthal burial: the skeletons of a woman and a small child.

This finding confirms a shift in human development. It makes it possible to conclude that Neanderthals cared for the dead. There is evidence and concern for patients.

It is believed that the Neanderthals gradually changed attitude to animals, they ceased to be a subject of hunting, but acquire meaning of the symbol, which linked the quiet life of a hunting team.

For example, in a small cave near Odessa discovered a huge cluster of cave bear bones - traces of a magical ritual Neanderthals. From the time of the Neanderthals survived and scoop mammoth, densely pomerezhena carved lines that seemed to outline the contours of the animal.

Thus, the Neanderthal era reaches the origin of art and religious beliefs. Cultivating Spirituality prorostaly among Neanderthals tens of thousands of years. However, archaeological findings also confirm the original savagery and cruelty. Thus, many findings give grounds to claim that among Neanderthals were widespread cannibalism.

When eluded especially great hunt, hunters united several communities. Links to other communities also supported the purpose of the formation of new families.
