The second half of 1919 was characterized by a sharp struggle in Ukraine between three forces: Red Army, White Army and the UPR. By mid-year, most of Ukraine controlled by the Bolsheviks. But their previous policy of "war communism" aroused the indignation of the population and, consequently, affected the combat readiness of the Red Army, stationed at the front.

In May 1919 the White forces of General Denikin, who controlled the Basin, Don, Kuban and North Caucasus, crossed a decisive offensive. Overcome the resistance of the Southern Front of the Red Army, they captured nearly all the Left-Bank Ukraine and moved further to Kyiv. While other parts of Denikin's army in accordance with the directive of 25 June 1919 to develop the offensive on Moscow. The only serious opposition armies of Denikin's troops committed N. Makhno.

Along the Right-bank Ukraine launched an offensive against the Bolsheviks of the UNR army.

It happened after July 17 Ukrainian Galician Army (UHA) and management ZUNR pressure Polish troops left East Galicia and crossed to the left bank of. Zbruch. According to the agreement between the governments of the UPR and UGA ZUNR joined the UNR Army to continue joint struggle for Ukraine's independence under the slogan "In Kiev Kyiv through!". The combined army numbered 80 thousand. People, of whom 50 thousand. Were Galicians. The combined army launched an offensive in two strategic areas: in Kyiv and Odesa. In Kyiv attacked Galicia, and to New York - part of the UNR army. Originally attack and has successfully developed 30 August Galicians came in Kyiv. Along the left bank of Dnipro in Kyiv to take part of the Volunteer Army under General delusional. Trying to come to terms with Denikin was not successful, and the Ukrainian part were forced to withdraw to Boryspil. Filing Kyiv was tantamount to defeat. This event became known as the "Kyiv catastrophe." Ukrainians have lost the character of an independent state. The moral and psychological defeat added and other problems: between Ukrainian and volunteer army fighting began, while the Polish army captured West dawdle, depriving the Ukrainian army rear; Army struck by an epidemic of typhus and other diseases and as a result, the amount was reduced by 70%.

The first armed resistance had ceased UGA, which after separate talks moved to the side of Denikin. E. Petrushevych went to Vienna. Directory collapsed. Army Petliura some time resisted. Once in the environment, the remains of the Ukrainian army switched to guerrilla fighting methods. Gen. M. Pavlenko Omelianovych-led troops raided parts of the UPR, which moved tylamy Denikin forces and the Red Army (the raid went down in history as the first "winter campaign" (December 1919 - May 1920)).

Petliura of the inner circle in December 1919 he went to Warsaw, where he began negotiations with the Polish authorities for recognition of the UPR and joint struggle against the Bolsheviks.

Historians estimate activity Petliura. Particular controversy is its agreement with Poland. But, despite this, Petliura go down in history as one of the leaders of the UPR and the national liberation movement.

In late summer 1919 Denikin occupied Ukraine and almost all of its territory was divided into three areas - Kharkov, Kiev and Novorossiysk. At the head of each executive standing with unlimited powers. Politics Denikin in the Ukraine was held under the banner of restoring "Great Russia". Ukrainian national movement was proclaimed treacherous and suffered persecution. The name "Ukraine" was banned. Violent repression awaited all who zapidozryuvavsya in opposition to the regime. The industry and the countryside began to revive the old order: the companies canceled all the gains of the revolution, the village restored landlord economy. With the punitive expeditions of the peasants confiscated buildings, implements, livestock. According to the law of the harvest in 1919 the peasants had to give one-third of the harvest to the landlords. Was also introduced tax - 5 pounds of grain from each tithing to the needs of the army. Delays and evasion is punishable by death. These orders led to an explosion of popular discontent, strikes and workers' uprising, the deployment of mass peasant movement (guerrilla movement units N. Makhno and other otamans), which Denikin could not cope. All Denikin's successes at the front against the Bolsheviks was reversed total collapse of their rear. Finally in October 1919 the Red Army inflicted on Denikin's counter-attack. After fierce fighting their main forces were defeated (Corps skins and Mammoth) and the remnants of Denikin, retreating at the beginning of 1920 entrenched in the Crimea. Denikin resigned from the duties of commander in chief. With the defeat of Denikin of the UGA find themselves in a difficult position, and in February 1920 sided with the Bolsheviks. The connection is called Red Ukrainian Galician Army (Chugay).

Thus, fighting the second half of 1919 led to the third coming of the Bolsheviks in the Ukraine. The main opponents of the Bolsheviks: UNR Army and Volunteer Army of General Denikin were defeated.
