Rivers of Ukraine

Each river has leaked - the place where it starts. Leaks can be a source, lake, swamp or ice in mountains. Some streams merge and give rise to the river. On its way it takes the water to other rivers - their tributaries. Burying through which the river flows is called a channel. The place where the river empties into another body of water - is her mouth. The rivers flowing into lakes, seas, oceans and other rivers.

Each river has left and right banks. To define them, you stand facing in the direction of flow. To the right is the right bank and left - left.

One of the great plains of rivers of Ukraine is the Dnieper. Most of its tributaries - Pripyat, Desna, Psyol Vorskla, Samara. Dnieper River originates in Russia, flows through Belarus.
