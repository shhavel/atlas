19th century entered the history of Ukraine as a period of national revival. Separated by boundaries of the two empires, the Ukrainian people have not lost the desire for freedom. Among educated Ukrainian revived interest in folk art, history and culture. There editions Ukrainian folk songs, thoughts, tales. After the publication of the "Aeneid" John Kotlyarevskogo developing new Ukrainian literature. Historians seek out and publish papers Ukrainian past. His enthusiasm Ukrainian movement owed constellation of talented individuals - Nicholas Kostomarov, Ivan Franko, Lesya Ukrainian. The symbol of Ukrainian revival was Taras Shevchenko.
