General Information

It lies in the west of Ukraine within the Ukrainian Carpathians and Carpathian Foredeep. By the relief is divided into three parts: northern - Podolsk Upland (up to 430 m), along the right bank of the Dniester - Carpathian lowland and mountain - Ukrainian Carpathians (almost 50% of the area). Mountains and ridges extend in a southeasterly direction and cut transverse and longitudinal valleys, Gorgan (g Syvulya, 1836 m), Pokutsko-Bukovina Carpathians; south - Hrynyavski and CHYVCHYNY mountains (Stop, 1684 m), the eastern slopes Chornogory (Goverla, 2061 m). The region is rich in oil and natural gas, lignite, bituminous mudstones. There are deposits of wax, sulfur, rock and potassium salts (saliferous Carpathian Basin), gypsum and anhydrite, shale, phosphate, building materials (sandstone, limestone, gravel, pebbles). 160 deposits of 24 kinds of minerals, opened more than 100 sources of mineral waters. The climate is continental. In the flat part of the winter is mild (-4 °, -4,5 ° in January), summers are warm (+ 18 °, + 19,5 °), precipitation of 600-800 mm. In the mountains the climate is much more severe temperature significantly decreases with height (in January -6 °, in July + 16 °), precipitation of 1400 mm. The main precipitation 65% - in the mountains (falls during the warm season). Summer rains sometimes lead to disastrous floods in the rivers. Rivers lot - 8286, but most of them are small, up to 10 km. They belong to the basin of the Dniester and Prut. The wooded area is the largest in Ukraine. Forests cover 626.6 thousand hectares. on the plains and foothills - 30% ( hornbeam-oak, oak, beech), on the slopes of the Carpathians - 60%. There is a high-altitude zone to 800-1 000 m slopes are covered with forests of beech, spruce, silver fir, 1 300-1 500 m - with cedar and spruce mountain pine. At the highest point - subalpine meadows - plains. There remained 120 species listed in the Red Book of Ukraine. Historical Background

At different periods in Precarpathians lived the Celts, Germans, Thracians, at the beginning of our era settled Slavs. Center of the region became the city of Galich, emerged 1100 years ago. From him and the land became known as Galicia. Even in the times of Kievan Rus' Galicia is the western outpost of the state, is soon formed a separate principality. In 1199 Prince Roman the Great united the Galician and Volyn principality. His son Daniel significantly expanded the borders of Galicia-Volyn principality. But in the XIV century. dilapidated state, and Galicia for four years became part of the Kingdom of Poland. In the XVII century. occurs city Stanislav (now Ivano-Frankivsk), and ancient Halych after 1240 declines and becomes a small town. Since 1772 Galicia was part of the Austrian Habsburg Empire (from 1867 - Austria-Hungary). After the collapse of Austria-Hungary Precarpathians is the cradle of Ukrainian national movement. In 1918, Galicia formed the West Ukrainian People's Republic. From January to May 1919 the government was in ZUNR Stanislav, and in September 1919 Galicia was occupied by Polish troops. By 1939, Stanislav was the center of the eponymous province within Poland. During the Second World War and in the years to Galicia in arms defending the right to existence of the Ukrainian state. In 1944 Stanislav region moves to the USSR. Since 1962 Stanislav renamed Ivano-Frankivsk.
