BACKGROUND AND TOP OF NATIONAL LIBERATION WAR UKRAINIAN people against Polish domination

Principal PROCEEDINGS

National Liberation War of the Ukrainian people against Polish domination in the middle of the XVII century. - The most significant event in the history of Ukraine, which marked the beginning of a new era in its history.

CHRONOLOGY OF EVENTS

1648, January - Beginning of the National Liberation War. The election of Hetman Bohdan Khmelnytsky.

1648, February - Bohdan Khmelnytsky entered into union agreement with the Crimean khan on providing military assistance.

1648, May - Zhovtovods'ka and Korsun battlefield.

NOTE!

The basis of rebel troops was the Cossacks, who played a leading role in the course of the war and the user. In the ranks of the rebels were many villagers and townspeople, as well as those from small Ukrainian Orthodox gentry. Supported the rebellion and Orthodox clergy.

The war had a nationalist, religious, feudal character.

The purpose of the National Liberation War was:

  • destruction of Polish rule;
  • the creation of ethnic boundaries Ukraine's own country;
  • elimination of serfdom, medium and large feudal land ownership;
  • establishment of Cossack type of management.

REASONS OF NATIONAL LIBERATION WAR

After the suppression of the Cossack rebellions of the first quarter of the XVII century. increased colonial policy of Poland towards Ukraine as caused an national liberation war.

1. The situation of peasants in terms of dominance magnates and corvee-filvarkova economic system:

  • rapid enslavement of the peasants;
  • increasing serfdom and various forms of refining and taxes for the benefit of landowners and the state;
  • enhance the exploitation of the peasants land lord's tenants.

2. Growing discontent Ukrainian bourgeoisie, which suffered both from private owners towns, and from tyranny royal officials:

  • Orthodox citizens had to settle only in designated neighborhoods;
  • limited in their classroom craft and trade;
  • removed from participation in local government, burdened by various taxes and levies.

3. Restrictions on the Rights of the Cossacks, the introduction of measures aimed at eliminating it as a state:

  • with cruelty suppressed any manifestations pokozachennya;
  • the "ordination ..." all the Cossacks, except 6000th registry - since parts of the Polish army - turned into serfs;
  • Registered denied the right to vote and Hetman - they had to assign the Polish government from the nobility;
  • located in Zaporozhye Polish garrison, the castle was rebuilt Kodak;
  • Cossacks prohibited without the consent of the government to go to the sea and the dry-valley campaign against the Ottoman Empire and Crimean Khanate. They are obliged to perform all kinds of work, pay onerous taxes.

4. Polonization Ukrainian culture, the forced imposition of Catholicism, which caused mass protests:

  • Orthodox obligated to pay taxes for the maintenance of the Catholic clergy; they are often forbidden to carry celebration, to occupy certain positions, to create higher education and so on;
  • Existing closed, forbidden to build new Orthodox churches and monasteries, and their possessions and property confiscated Catholic and Uniate clergy; instead, built churches and the Monastery;
  • increasing the number of Jesuit colleges, spread their influence;
  • narrowed the scope of use of the Ukrainian language.

Therefore, the measures that the Polish government was accompanied by his dominance in the Ukrainian lands were to complete their conquest and subjugation, that were colonial. The colonial policy of the Government of the Commonwealth in the middle of the XVII century. exacerbated the situation in Ukraine, causing a nationwide speech.

Bogdan Khmelnitsky

War of the Ukrainian people against the domination of the Commonwealth led by Bohdan Khmelnytsky.

He was born December 27, 1595 in a family of minor gentry Ukrainian Mykhailo Khmelnytsky on the family estate on the farm Saturday to Chyhyryn.

He studied at the school at one of the monasteries in Kyiv and in the Jesuit College Lviv, therefore, on the one hand, took a traditional Ukrainian education, and the other - joined the European ideas of the West. This combination of armed Khmelnytsky extensive knowledge of languages ​​- Portrait Ukrainian, Polish, Latin, and helped to shape the future outlook of the hetman.

After returning from the city Bohdan Khmelnytsky signed Chiguirin hundreds of registered and already in 1620 in its stock went on a campaign against the Turks in Moldavia. There, at the Battle of Tsetsorskyh fields captured.

Two years was in Turkish captivity, where studied Turkish and Tatar languages. Freed from captivity and returned to serve in Chigirinskogo Regiment.

Participated in the Smolensk War 1632-1634 biennium. On the side of Poland, in the naval campaigns against Turkey, the Cossack uprising 1637-1638 biennium.

We perform various diplomatic missions. Specifically, in December 1638 as part of the embassy, ​​which was to achieve mitigation "ordination ...", met with the Polish king. During the 1646-1647 biennium. Again met with Polish King Wladyslaw IV. During the meeting the right Cossack involvement in hostilities against Poland Ottoman Empire.

As the facts show, Bohdan Khmelnytsky had already prepared a rebellion against Poland. To that end, he tried to use the plans of the Polish court on the war against Turkey, in particular voyage Cossacks into the Polish army. On plans Khmelnytsky learned Polish magnates, as evidenced by the events of the personal life of the hetman. Disputes Chygyryns'ka pidstarostoyu Daniel Chaplynka through the estate on Saturday, which he would select, Khmelnytsky led the summer of 1646 in Warsaw. We know that from the King Bogdan received confirmation of their rights to Saturday. However, this did not stop Chaplinsky. In early 1647 he ordered his son was beaten Khmelnitsky in the spring of that year took Chaplinsky Saturday and drove out family Khmelnitsky. So Khmelnytsky on personal experience convinced that tyranny magnates and nobility in the Commonwealth has no limits, and no one but the Cossacks, not protect the rights of the wicked people. So he and his closest associates are actively preparing a rebellion. The intention was discovered, and the fall 1647 Khmelnytsky arrested. In mid-December it with a small detachment of Cossacks and son Timosha went to Zaporozhye.

PREPARATION armed uprisings against the Commonwealth

In early 1648 Bohdan Khmelnytsky formed the first rebel, established relationship with the Cossacks of the garrison on January 25 and Host mastered it. He was elected hetman of the Zaporozhian Host. These events marked the beginning of the National Liberation War of the Ukrainian people.

Activities of Bohdan Khmelnytsky preparation armed speech:

  • universal sent to the Ukrainian people with appeals to join the Cossack troops .;
  • established production of gunpowder, organized the purchase of arms and ammunition;
  • from the beginning of hostilities reached an agreement on the transfer of registered Cossacks to the rebels;
  • negotiated with Turkey and especially - with the Crimean Khanate.

With the support of Turkey, in February and March 1648 in Bakhchisarai Ukrainian ambassadors made a deal with the Crimean Khan Islam-III Ґireyem military assistance in the war against the Commonwealth.

Thus Hetman solved the problem:

  • absence of their own cavalry;
  • avoid sudden attack Tatar and Nogai hordes that would be particularly desirable during the war against Poland.

GETTING armed struggle against the Commonwealth

Zhovtovods'ka battle (5-6 May 1648)

The first battles of the National Liberation War. The rebels surrounded the Polish army under the command of Stefan Potocki on the banks of the Yellow River Water. The decisive attack on the Polish camp began on 6 May. He ended with a crushing defeat of the Poles.

Korsun battle (May 16, 1648)

After the defeat at Yellow Waters 20,000th Polish army led by the great crown hetman Mikolaj Potocki and Hetman Pol'na Martin Kalinowski went to Korsun, which disappeared in a fortified camp. May 14 troops approached the city B.Khmelnytskyi (Cossacks and Tatars Perekop nobleman Tugay Bay). Pretending preparing to storm the camp, Khmelnytsky led the Polish army move to Bohuslav. In the swampy tract Pea Dubrava May 16 the Polish army, which was moving camp, came upon the ambush (6000th Cossack detachment Maxim Krivonosa). Simultaneously attack the main force Khmelnitsky squad Krivonosa Polish army was defeated.

Victory at Yellow Waters and Korsun was the signal for uprisings throughout Ukraine and Ukrainian Liberation Army march westward.

Bohdan Khmelnytsky troops gathered at the White Church, formed the new Ukrainian authorities and administer deliverance from the forces of the Right-Bank Ukraine the Commonwealth entrusted to Colonel Maxim Krivonosa.

By the end of July from the Polish yoke was released the entire territory of the Left Bank, and by the end of August - Bratslav, Kiev, Podolsk (except m. Kamenetz) Province on the right bank, and the eastern and southern regions of Volyn province.
