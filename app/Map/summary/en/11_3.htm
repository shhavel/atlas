Ukraine in World War II (June 1941 - November 1942)

The plan of attack on the Soviet Union, which was named "Barbarossa" was signed by Hitler in December 1940 in its calculations came from the German command of a lightning strike that anticipated a quick and complete victory over the enemy during the campaign.

Implementation plan "Barbarossa" began at dawn June 22, 1941 Three of the army - "Midnight", "Center" and "South" - a total of about 5.5 million people, while they led the attack, respectively, through the Baltic states to Leningrad, through Belarus to Moscow, and Kyiv. Are combined with the German army in the attack on the Ukraine participated in the German army allies - Romania and Hungary.

Despite the militaristic nature of the Soviet state, before the war it was not ready. Already in the first days of fighting, the Red Army suffered heavy losses and began to retreat. Army Group "South" in Ukraine opposing forces Kiev Special District and Odessa Military District. For a more effective defense of their reorganized in Southwestern Front (commanded by Michael Kyrponos) and the Southern Front (Commander John Tyulenyev). Desperate attempt to stop the enemy in the vicinity of Brody-Lutsk-Rovno made armored units of the Southwestern Front. A few days (23-27 June) fierce tank battles, but despite the loss, the enemy continued to attack.

In August, the enemy moved to Dnipro and Black Sea. The fighting took place on the outskirts of Kyiv. In a tight siege was Odessa. Around the capital of Ukraine was established three lines of defense all the attacks on the city from the west were repulsed. Moreover, the aggressor has lost about 100 thousand. People. For a while the enemy attempts to seize Kyiv stopped using frontal impact.

Deep tank breakthrough in late July the Germans were able to surround the area in Uman of the 6th and 12th Army of the Southwestern Front. Please continue this strategic maneuver command of the Wehrmacht used during the entire period of advance.

In the battles lost colonel general Kyrponos Chief of Staff Michael Burmystrenko front and many other senior military leaders. September 19th the Germans entered Kyiv.

The heavy defeat near Kyiv Ukraine gave into the hands of enemies. During October the Nazis took over Left. October 24, Soviet troops left Kharkov, 26 - Stalin (Donetsk) .15 October prypynalasya heroic defense of Odessa. Evacuation surrounded on all sides by the city carried by sea.

By mid-November was occupied Crimea. Kept only Sevastopol - the main base of the Soviet Black Sea Fleet. All attempts to take the city by storm for the Germans ended in failure and heavy losses.

In late autumn German forces controlled large expanse of Soviet territory indeed began to show the first signs of exhaustion victorious offensive. So in November - December 1941 failed operation to capture Moscow. December 5 at Moscow's Red Army passed in attacks and threw the enemy at a distance of from 100 to 250 km. Thus, the plan "Barbarossa" was frustrated. Building on the success, began to counterattack and the South-West and South fronts. In January 1942 power 6, 12, 21 and 58th armies took the offensive in the area Raisins - Barvinkove - Lozovaja to regain Kharkiv, enter the Dnieper and eliminate Kharkov and Donbas enemy. But, having moved west at 90 km advancing troops stopped for lack of effort. However, it was possible to keep the bridgehead, the so-called "Barvenkovsky performance."

In Crimea, before the new year 1942 in parts of the 44th and 55th armies and ships of the Black Sea Fleet amphibious operation conducted for the release of the Kerch Peninsula. On these bridgehead deployed forces Crimean front. Amphibious operation of the distracted enemy troops from the siege of Sevastopol.

In April 1942 active hostilities stopped. The situation stabilized at the front and both sides began to prepare for the summer campaign. With renewed vigor the war broke out in May. The Soviet leadership expected that the Nazis resort to a new effort in the Moscow area. It is a mistake calculating the balance of forces in the south, Stalin ordered the attack near Kharkov. It turned into a brutal defeat of the South-Western Front, they were commanded by Marshal Semyon Timoshenko. Blow from the south and north Germans were in the grip of the 6th, 9th and 57th armies advancing from Barvinkivsky offensive. In captivity got 240 thousand. Red (29 May). In Crimea, May 8 went on the offensive 11th German Army. By May 20 the Germans captured Kerch again, causing major losses to Soviet troops and forced them to retreat to the mainland. The defeat of the Red Army in the Crimea decided the fate of Sevastopol. July 4th after 8 months of resistance defenders left the city.

After a decisive victory at Kharkov and Crimea, June 28, the Germans moved into large-scale offensive in the southern sector of the German-Soviet front. In July, the occupation was completed Donbass. 22 of this month the Germans entered the city of Sverdlovsk Voroshylovhrad (Luhansk) region, the latest in Ukraine. At the same time it was occupied by the right bank of the Don. Next 17th field and 1 Panzer Army led Hitler to attack the Caucasus and 6th Field and 4th Panzer - fought out on the Volga at Stalingrad. The battles of Stalingrad in August - November 1942 have been crucial in the war. After losing the city would cut off the Caucasus from the main part of the Soviet Union, and therefore - would be lost oil fields in Azerbaijan. Besides, would open the way to the Urals - the main material basis of the Soviet defense.
