According to the national (ethnic) population structure, all countries are divided into mononational and multinational. Mononational countries appear at the coincidence of ethnic and political boundaries. Such countries are predominant in Europe, Latin America, South-West Asia. In a world dominated by multinational countries (India, Pakistan, China, Russia, the US, Indonesia, etc.). Belong to this group and binational countries: Belgium, Canada.  For multinational characteristic of a federal administrative-territorial units. Unitary countries are generally mononational.
