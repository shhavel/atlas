EMERGENCE OF UKRAINIAN Cossacks and Zaporozhian Sich

Principal PROCEEDINGS

The appearance of the Cossacks was caused by both internal and external factors and showed great vitality of contemporary Ukrainian. Provided that threatened the very existence of the Ukrainian people, Cossacks gradually become a force that can defend their homeland from attacks by strangers, to preserve ethnic identity, as well as their own state.

ZaporizhzhyaJanuary - Orthodox Christian democratic republic.

ZaporizhzhyaJanuary played a crucial role in the history of the Ukrainian people, especially in its national liberation struggle, the development of Ukraine's armed forces and military art in the development and settlement of vast steppes of Central and Southern Ukraine.

The consequence of economic activity Cossacks was the colonization of the Wild Fields (steppe between the Dniester and the Don), thus, expanding the living space of the Ukrainian people, which took place peacefully, without conquests foreign territories.

Last march Dmitry Vishnevetskogo began in the summer of 1563 with a detachment of Cossacks moved to the Prince of Moldavia. It was captured and soon was transferred to the Turks. CAUSES OF UKRAINIAN Cossacks

Predatory raids of Crimean and Sultan hordes of troops.

Caught in circumstances that threatened the existence of the Ukrainian nation as a separate, Ukrainian had to learn complex military profession. The constant battle against the attackers produced from the farmer, craftsman, priest, a special type of soldier - brave and cunning that a good command of various weapons, full of freedom and ever ready to protest against any coercion.

The spread of serfdom and the introduction of serfdom led to massive Ukrainian peasants flee - to the end of the world, if only out of exploiters. Difficult conditions border fugitives seemed less a disaster than the loss of personal freedom.

BACKGROUND

Crimean peninsula in the Black Sea and Azov steppes had long been inhabited. Before the Mongol invasion of the Black Sea steppes belonged Polovtsy - Turkic nomads. Conquered them, the Mongol khans annexed their lands to their immediate possessions. Actually, if the Russian lands and forest-steppe Polesie were forced to acknowledge the supremacy of the Horde, but its territory is not considered, because they were not suitable for nomadic pastoralism - the main lesson Horde, the territories of the Tartar Steppe were ground, or Tartary, as saying at that time. After the Tartars called the new conquerors of our ancestors. So for the Turkic population of the Black Sea stuck the name of his conquerors. In the XV century. with the intensification of the struggle for power in the Golden Horde rulers Crimean ulus - was the name of that part of the Golden Horde khans of their possessions - and began to seek a one-man rule. Year 1449 Haji Ґirey declared himself an independent ruler of the Crimean Khanate. The capital of the state he chose Bakhchisaray (formerly the center of the Crimean ulus was m. Crimea). Thus, the Crimean Khanate as an independent feudal state was formed due to the collapse of the Golden Horde and involved the Crimean peninsula, the lower reaches of the Dnieper, Azov and Kuban.

1475 directly to the borders of the Crimean Khanate pidsunulysya possession of Turkey, the border between the states had been laid by the Black and Azov Seas. This happened as a result of the conquest of Turkey - at that time it was called the Ottoman Empire - the Black Sea port cities. Turkey during the XIV - XVI centuries. from a small principality became a powerful empire which stretched possession on three continents (Asia, Africa and Europe) from the Atlantic to the Indian Ocean.

Considering the military power of the Sultan of Turkey, the Crimean khan Mengli Ґirey 1478 preferred to be his vassal. Vassalage was detected mainly in the fact that Khan had to take part in military campaigns Sultan.

Change in foreign policy orientations of the Crimean Khanate resulted in devastating hordes of predatory raids of Crimean Ukrainian lands.

For several decades the Ukrainian lands have become a major source of supply of slaves on the slave markets of the Crimea and Turkey, where they were sold to the most remote corners of Asia.

FORMATION Cossack state - Zaporizhzhya Sich

During the XVI - XVIII centuries. ZaporizhzhyaJanuary had signs state: it controlled a vast territory steppe Ukraine had its own government, military and administrative structure, its own judgment, independent diplomatic relations with other countries.

ZaporizhzhyaJanuary different from most contemporary feudal states. Instead feudal coercion, which was based on the enslavement and forced labor, it affirmed the principles of wage labor. For internal build Cossack state was kind of a military company.

The area was called the Cossack state land Zaporozhian Cossack Army or liberties and stretched from the Southern Bug in the west to Kalmius Nadazov`yi in the east. In the north, its boundary ran along the rivers eagle (the Left) and upper Ingula and Inhulets (On the Right Bank).

Authorities in Cossack state

All power in the Cossack territory belonged Urinary (military) Council, which elected Cossack military officers.

COSSACK SYMBOLS

Coat of Arms of the Cossack Army - the figure of a Cossack armed with a musket and sword - emerged in the second half of the XVI century. He came into force under King Stefan Batory 1578 But prior Cossack formation probably had their own symbols. However, the information about it to our time not reached. Said emblem was first depicted on the seal of the Zaporozhian Host in 1595, Hetman Gregory Loboda. Throughout the existence of the principal symbol of the Cossacks remained unchanged. He was represented at the main seals and Hetman Cossack Army, on flags, portraits, icons, manuscripts and printed editions.
