In the modern world the state can not exist in isolation. Between them is a versatile communication and relationships (economic, military, financial, human, etc.). In order to regulate interstate relations established international organizations. In the world today there are several thousand of them. According to the purpose of international organizations are divided into general political (UN, NAM, EU, CIS) and special designed to solve specific problems:

  • military (NATO, ANZUS);
  • economic (ASEAN IOP, OPEC, OECD, NAFTA);
  • financial (World Bank, IMF);
  • intellectual and analytical (Club of Rome).

Some international organizations are global, others - regional.

The largest and most influential international organization is the United Nations - global general political organization established in 1945 to support and strengthen international peace, security and development cooperation between the countries.

The main bodies of the UN General Assembly, Security Council, Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), the International Court. Special UN bodies on specific issues are UNESCO, FAO, WHO.

Regional organizations formed by groups of certain regions of the world coming together to solve a variety of tasks on the basis of common interests.

Ukraine as a respected European country is a member of a number of influential international organizations and seeks full integration into Euro-Atlantic institutions (EU, NATO).
