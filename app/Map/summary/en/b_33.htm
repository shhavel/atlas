Industrial crops - a plant that is the raw material for further processing in various industries. According to the purpose industrial crops are divided into fibrous, oil, sugar, tonic, starchy, elastica, drugs and others. Growing crops is very time-consuming and costly (requires the introduction of a large quantity of fertilizers, irrigation, etc.). Because they are grown, mostly in developing countries or countries the average level of development where even with extensive farming in the presence of low-cost labor force, growing crops is profitable.

Among the fiber crops (cotton, flax, jute, kenaf) is the most common cotton. With him produce many kinds of fabrics, wool, synthetic felt; the seed - oil; waste is used as animal feed. Cotton - the culture of tropical and subtropical climatic zones. The largest producers and exporters of cotton are China, India, USA, Pakistan, Uzbekistan, Australia, Brazil, Turkey, Africa.

Flax cold-resistant culture. It is grown in Europe and China. In the United States, Canada, Argentina, India is grown mainly oil varieties of flax (flax).

The most common oil crops are soybeans, sunflower, peanut, canola, olive, coconut palm oil. Soybeans are grown primarily in the United States, China, Brazil, Indonesia, peanuts - in South Asia, West Africa and Argentina.

Sweets crops are sugar cane (it accounts for 60% of world production of sugar) and sugar beets (40%). The main exporters of cane sugar - Brazil, Mexico, Cuba, Jamaica, Australia.

Sugar beets are grown in countries with relatively high agriculture (proper rotation, high levels of chemicals and mechanization of production) and developed livestock that provide high yields and using waste sugar beet. The main producers of beet sugar is Ukraine, France, Russia.

Tonic crops (tea bush, coffee and kakaove tree) - a plant of the tropics and subtropics. The largest suppliers of tea leaves in the world market are India, China, Sri Lanka; coffee beans - Brazil, Colombia; fruit cocoa - Ivory Coast, Brazil, Ghana.

The potato is the most common of bulboplodnyh cultures. This is a valuable food crops, which is a raw material in starch and syrup and alcohol industry. By bulboplodnyh used as food crops, are also sweet (sweet potato), yams, cassava, taro.

Cultural plantations of rubber trees, which is the raw material for the production of natural rubber, prevalent in South and Southeast Asia (Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand).
