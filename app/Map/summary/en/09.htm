Religion is an important part of the spiritual life of mankind, is largely determined by the formation of mentality and values ​​of individual nations, but also affects the political and socio-economic status of entire countries and regions.

The most common and influential religions in the world are Christianity, Islam and Buddhism. They are called world religions. In addition, there are national religion of the world, who are ethnically defined nations (Hinduism, Judaism, Shinto, etc.).

Most numerous in the number of believers is Christianity, there are 2 thousand years. Christian faith professes almost a third of the world population, mainly in Europe, America and Australia. Christianity is divided into three trends: Catholicism, Orthodoxy and Protestantism. Today 60% of all Christians of the world - Roman Catholics. Single center of the Catholic Church is the Vatican, where the permanent residence of the head of the Church - the Pope.

Orthodoxy - for Christianity, formed after the separation of the churches in 1054. In the Orthodox faith no single center, however there autocephalous national churches, led by the patriarch.

Among followers of Protestantism stand out: Lutherans, Anglicans, Baptists, Adventists, Mormons, etc.

The second in the number of believers and the youngest world religion - Islam is also not uniform. Adherents of Islam (Muslims) are divided into Sunnis and Shiites. The Sunnis, in contrast to the Shiites, along with the Koran - the holy book of Islam - the source of faith believe the Sunnah - the scripture of the Prophet Muhammad.

The third in the number of believers world religion - Buddhism unites supporters of the narrow path (Hinayana) and admirers of the great path (Mahayana).

The largest by number of adherents among the national religions is Hinduism. It exists more than 5000 years and recognizes polytheism. Among the major Hindu gods - Brahma, Shiva, Vishnu, Kali.

Judaism originated in Palestine 4 thousand years ago. Jews believe in one God, indicated by the great prophets in their revelations. At one time, this belief gave rise to Christianity and Islam.

Chinese is the national religion of Confucianism and Taoism. Ancient cults of nature and the ancestors of the Japanese shape in modern Shinto.

Traditional local beliefs, the so-called primitive religions profess the aborigines of Australia, the separate tribes of Asia and Africa, the Indians of America, the peoples of the Russian North.
