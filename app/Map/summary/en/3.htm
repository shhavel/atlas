Immediately after the First World War Ukrainian land turned into a theater of war or front-line zone between the Russian army and the troops of the Austro-German bloc. On this site acted Russian Southwestern Front, which in 1916 added more and Romanian. In August - September 1914 Russian troops repulsed the attack of the Austro-Hungarian army, directed the shot to Galicia. This operation is called Galician battle. As a result of this grand battle the Russian army captured the Eastern Galicia, northern Bukovina, and came to the Carpathian passes. Russian troops entered Lviv, Chernivtsi. During the battle, the Austro-Hungarian forces lost 400 thousand. People, of which 100 thousand. Captured. Only because of urgent assistance from Germany and the transfer of troops from other fronts of Austria-Hungary escaped final defeat.

Another success of the Russian troops was the siege of the fortress of Przemysl, which surrendered March 22, 1915 As a result, captured another 120 thousand. Austrians and captured 400 guns. The conquered territories Russian government established a regime that, according to the head of the Russian administration in Galicia Governor-General Count Alexander Bobrinsky had to introduce "n principle" in the life of the province's population. Thus, in Galicia closed Ukrainian schools and other educational institutions, cultural organizations, introduced the Russian language and Russian legislation. It began inversion Greek Catholics to Orthodoxy. Almost all Ukrainian elites who remained in the land, and the tip of the Greek Catholic Church, led by Andrey Sheptyts'kyi taken deep into Russia. There were pogroms.

Russian occupation lasted until the spring - summer 1915 In February of Austrian came to Chernivtsi, June 22 - to the city, and by the end of June have captured all of Galicia and Bukovina, with the exception of a narrow strip east of Ternopil. Apart from Galicia, Austro-German forces during the offensive in 1915 occupied Volhynia, Kholmshchyna Beresteyschina cities of Lutsk and Dubno. The onset of the Austro-German army halted on the line Kamenetz-Podolsk - Stockholm - Kremenets - Dubno.

The retreat of the Russian army was accompanied by new riots Ukrainians, the deportation of the population. At the same time to the retreating Russian units were added refugees who fear persecution on the part of Austria-Hungary. In general, Russia has been moved more than 100 thousand. Ukrainian. Deputies of the State Duma Miliukov described Russian policy in Galicia "European scandal."

In the spring of 1916 the troops of the Southwestern Front under A. Brusilov launched a surprise attack, which went down in history as Brusilovsky breakthrough. Russian troops launched an offensive May 22, 1916 after a short artillery barrage along the entire front moved inland 80 - 120 km, again captured Chernivtsi, Coloma, Brody Luck. Because Brusilovsky breakthrough Austro-Hungarian troops suffered heavy losses: over 1 million dead and wounded more than 400 thousand. War. Russian forces lost about 500 thousand. People.

Upon completion of this offensive front line remained almost unchanged throughout the year. Meanwhile, in March 1917 there was a revolution in Russia. The new Russian government in an effort to strengthen the internal situation of the country in the summer of 1917 began a new offensive, which ended in failure and new victims. Counterstrike Austro-German troops forced the Russian troops leave Galicia, withdraw even further than in 1915. This front line lasted until the conclusion of the Brest Peace (January 1918).
