VI-VII centuries. were a difficult period in the history of the Frankish kingdom. Countries covered the bloody wars between the heirs Hlodviґa who tried to destroy each other in a battle for the crown. During the second half of VI. lasted four decades of war between two branches of the dynasty Merovinґiv. However Hlodviґa descendants continued conquest of neighboring lands.

In the second half of VI. Frankish kingdom declined: Merovinґy still held the throne, but in fact it solved royal governors - mazhordomy. This time is called the era of lazy kings. From major-domo Charles Martel (715-741 gg.) Led the fight against the Arabs who from Spain, the Pyrenees, attacked the kingdom. Through his decisive action was set up well armed cavalry and defeat the Arabs at Poitiers 732. Son of Charles Martel Pepin the Short (741-768 gg.) Is no longer satisfied mazhordoma post. The new dynasty was called Pipinidiv or Carolingian, by the name of its most prominent representative, the son of Pepin the Short - Charlemagne (768-814 gg.).
