The lack of credible data on Krivonosa explained perhaps by the fact that for a long time, he served as a mercenary somewhere in foreign armies, and in Ukraine appeared on the eve of the Liberation War. Maxim Kryvonis consistently opposed any agreement with the Government of the Commonwealth, for the complete liberation of Ukraine. He died in November 1648 in a camp near Zamosc from the plague.

Battle of Pyliavtsi

Pylyavetska battle took place 11-13 September 1648

New Union of Cossack-Tatar army led by Bohdan Khmelnytsky coincides with the Polish Army under the command of Hetman Potocki Nicholas nearby town Pyliavtsi near Starokonstantinov (now the village Piliava Khmelnitsky region.).

The central point of the battle was a dam across the river. On the first day it twice passed from hand to hand, but on the third attempt, the Poles managed to grab it.

The decisive battle began on 13 September. On the morning of Ukrainian, Crimean Tatar and Nogai groups lined up battle formations. Host Khmelnytsky powerful blow repulsed dam. Soon, Cossacks began to pursue the enemy. Polish command failed to provide an organized retreat. Abandoning weapons and ammunition left, Polish army nakyvalo heels.

Pylyavetska battle ended for the Polish Army in ignominious defeat.

Resume hostilities in 1649 Zbarazkiy Zboriv CAMPAIGN

In the spring of 1649 the Polish troops, violating the armistice began the hostilities.

Polish troops to speak against the Cossacks gathered under Starokonstantinov. The news that there is about to go army Khmelnytsky was a surprise to the Polish command. Poles decided to retreat.

A placement Polish troops were elected Zbraj. Some groups took Zbarazkiy castle, and the rest began to erect near the camp. And end it did not have time for June 30 came Ukrainian and Tatar cavalry. Flared battle. Zbraj and Polish camp under its walls were taken in a tight siege.

July 27 Cossacks launched a massive offensive. The battle lasted three hours; Poles fiercely boronylysya. The next day the Khmelnytsky received messages from Warsaw Zbraj sailed under main Polish army led by Jan Kazimierz. Considering this fact, hetman decided to change the course of military operations. The bulk of the Cossack army, led by Khmelnytsky, unbeknownst to the enemy withdrew from the Zbarazh and moved towards the king.

The conclusion of the Treaty of Zboriv

August 8, 1649

Zborowski Treaty

The contract called for passage under the hetman former territories of Kiev, Chernigov and Bratslav provinces.

In these areas, the Polish government was deprived of the right to place their troops, and to hold public office had only Orthodox.

Established the registry in 40 thousand. Cossacks.

All participants declared war amnesty.

Gentry returned to their possessions.

The peasants had to perform duties in the pre-war estates of their owners.

Place Zborivsky agreement in the events of the National Liberation War

Zborowski peace treaty concluded under pressure corresponded to the actual success of the National Liberation War.

However, it was of great importance to the rise of the idea of ​​Ukrainian statehood, for the first time in the history of Ukrainian-Polish relations Ukraine received from Poland recognizing certain autonomy as a Cossack state. It gave to the Hetman's government to continue the struggle for independence.

However Zborowski agreement did not satisfy either the Poles or Ukrainian Poles felt they gave too many Cossacks and the Cossacks were convinced that got small. The war stopped only temporarily.
