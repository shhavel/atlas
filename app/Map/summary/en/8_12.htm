FORMATION OF UKRAINIAN Cossack state - Hetmanshchyna

Principal PROCEEDINGS

The main achievement of the National Liberation War - strengthening Ukrainian state - Hetman.

Feature of the Cossack state was its military. It is explained by a need to fight for independence and traditions of the Zaporozhian Host.

During the summer - fall 1648 to liberated Ukrainian lands was established Ukrainian central and local government, the judiciary, introduced a new principle of administrative-territorial division, gradually formed a new socio-economic structure.

To participate in the development of the state were involved in various segments of contemporary Ukrainian society (Cossacks, Ukrainian Orthodox gentry, burgesses and clergy).

Administrative division

The territory of the Cossack state under the terms of the Treaty of Zboriv consisted of the former lands of Kiev, Chernigov and Bratslav provinces and held 200 thousand sq. M. km. - From p. Sluch in the west to the east border of Moscow and of Pripyat basin in the north to the south of the steppe zone.

The capital and residence of the Hetman was m. Chyhyryn.

In the liberated lands abolished Polish administrative system. It eliminated the province, counties, and instead created its territorial regiments of the division. In 1649 the entire territory of the Ukrainian state was divided into 16 bands (on the Right Bank - 9, on the Left Bank - 7 bands).

Center regiment was one of the great cities of the regimental area.

Each regiment led by the colonel, who was elected to the board or appointed regimental hetman. Colonel concentrated in the hands of the military, judicial and administrative authorities in the regiment, that was not just a military leader, but has power over all the inhabitants of the regiment.

The territory of the regiment was divided into 10-20 and even more hundreds. Hundreds like shelves, varied size and strength. Administrative centers were hundreds of cities, towns and large villages.

Military and administrative power in the hundreds of hundreds performed.

The city, which had the Magdeburg Law (Kyiv, Nizhin, Chernihiv, Pereyaslav, Starodub, Deaf, Poltava, Baturin et al.), Directed by magistrates led by enter. In the villages the headman in charge of affairs, which chose farming community and the affairs of the Cossacks - their elected chiefs.

Zaporizhzhya Sich was in a state separate administrative unit.

CREATION OF UKRAINIAN ARMY

Hetman state was one of the strongest armies in Europe at that time, which was created by the efforts of Bohdan Khmelnytsky and the Cossack officers experienced in the first year of the war associations scattered peasant and Cossack troops.

People's Liberation Army had more than 100 thousand. Soldiers and was organized by the regimental-sotnia territorial principle: certain territory exhibited several hundred Cossacks, who rallied the regiment.

The army consisted of representatives from different segments of the population. Most were farmers and tradesmen pokozachene. However, the core of the army was Registered and Zaporozhye Cossacks.

Structuring the army, his financial support, training and personnel policies as implemented in the tradition of the Cossack Army.

The basis of the Cossack troops was infantry. On the initiative of the hetman during the National Liberation War was created Cossack cavalry, already in 1649 successfully resisted the enemy. Before the army also included troops intelligence services and border fortification, detachments of the Guard. Special teams ensure the supply of arms, ammunition and food.

The organization of the state apparatus, maintenance of troops, diplomacy require considerable resources. Money matters in the Cossack territory was directly supervised Bogdan Khmelnitsky.

There are several sources of revenue Military treasure, and above all - the land that passed into use treasure agricultural industries (mills, breweries, etc.) and payment for their rent.

PROCEDURE

In the Cossack state operated its own system of justice. It consisted of the General Court, regimental and centesimal courts.

The highest judicial institution was a general court-martial hetman. He considered the appeal proceedings in military courts and centesimal, and some things with which petitioners appealed directly to the hetman.

The General Court consisted of two master judges and court clerk.

Courts at the local level headed person colonels or captains. Of Cossack ships were subject not only to the Cossacks, but the townspeople and peasants.

In the cities of Magdeburg Law were municipal courts. In addition, Ukraine had also ecclesiastical courts, the validity of which was distributed to the internal affairs of the clergy.

Changes in socio-economic life of the Cossack UKRAINE

The promotion of Ukrainian Cossack state - Hetman - has been accompanied by profound changes in the economic and social life.

It eliminated the large and medium secular tenure, filvarkova-corvee system of economic serfdom.

Instead formed Cossacks, peasants, and public ownership of land.

In connection with this change of social structure. Were forced to leave Ukraine Polish magnates and gentry Catholic, Catholic clergy - those groups that were holding in their hands before the start of the National Liberation War.

Leading role in society was to play a Cossack state.

The government and the main wealth concentrated in the hands of the Cossack.

Cossack officers formed of representatives of different social groups.

An absolute conquest of the Cossack state was the personal freedom of the absolute majority of peasants and burghers, which, moreover, began to flock to the Cossack.

The vast majority of farmers pokozachylasya began to conduct free Cossack economy.

The position of the middle class is also improved because the city had eliminated the dominance of foreigners and remove national and religious barriers to employment in crafts, fishing, trade, to participate in government.
