Asia - the largest in the world. It is home to more than half of humanity. Her vast expanses of ancient civilizations emerged with high material and spiritual culture, there emerged three world religions, and now formed the specific model of socio-economic development: Japanese, Chinese model of Petroleum Exporting Countries, etc.

In terms of economic development of most countries included in the group of developing countries, and Japan, and Israel is highly developed. North Korea, Vietnam and China belong to the group of countries with centrally controlled economy. The Caucasus and Central Asia - transitional economy.

For economy of most countries in Asia are characterized by the predominance of agriculture over industry and agriculture dominates crop.

For industry (excluding Japan, China, Kazakhstan, NEC) are characterized by the dominance of mining industry of cutting.

The international division of labor to Asia, with the exception of developed, are suppliers of agricultural products (tea, natural rubber, cotton, spices, tropical and subtropical fruits) and of mining and extractive industries (oil, gas, manganese, tin, chromite, iron ore, etc.).

South-East Asia - most of the Middle East (from Turkey to Afghanistan and the island of Cyprus) This includes 16 independent states and the Palestinian territories. This region is called crossroads because it is located at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, Africa, the crossroads of major shipping lanes. This situation and the presence of large oil deposits (sq. / 3 of world reserves) and gas (1/4 of world reserves) give the region an important economic and strategic importance.

The economy of the region is mainly agrarian and raw nature. In the main oil-producing countries, state and nature of the economy depends on the production and marketing of oil and gas (Iraq, Iran, Bahrain, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, UAE, Saudi Arabia). In some countries has significantly developed petrochemical industry, some engineering and metalworking (Israel, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Turkey). Problems Southwest Asia are water shortages and political instability.

West Asia (Caucasus) - located in the southern part of the Caucasus Isthmus (Armenia, Azerbaijan, Georgia). By its geographic location, these countries belong to Asia, but the story development and cultural traditions (Georgia and Armenia - Christian countries), some scientists consider them to European countries. These states have close ties with both Central and Eastern Europe and the countries of South-West Asia. Caucasus is rich in fuel and energy resources (Azerbaijan), iron ore (Azerbaijan, Armenia), manganese ore (Georgia), copper ore (Armenia and Georgia). This region is known as a resort and one that specializes in growing vegetables, fruit, citrus, grapes and tea.

Central Asia. To this region of Mongolia, Kazakhstan and Central Asia - Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan. These countries are located in the drainage area of ​​Eurasia in the area of ​​deserts and semi-deserts. One of the main problems of the region is a significant shortage of water. Countries in the region are well equipped with several types of minerals: coal (Kazakhstan), oil and natural gas (Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan), ferrous metals (Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan) and chemical raw materials. Mining industry is the leading industry in most countries. In the economic sector of Kazakhstan allocated metallurgy, chemical industry, machinery, light industry and food processing. Agricultural region specializes in growing corn and cotton, dairy and beef cattle, horse breeding (Mongolia).

To the Southeast Asian peninsula include Indochina, Malay Archipelago and the Philippine Islands. In this region - 11 countries: Myanmar, Vietnam, Laos, East Timor, Thailand, Philippines, Cambodia, Malaysia, Brunei, Indonesia, Singapore. Because of Southeast Asia are way from Europe and the Middle East to Asia and Australia. Countries in the region are well equipped with natural resources, with a strong export base and became the largest and sometimes monopolistic exporters of certain goods (consumer electronics, telecommunications equipment, integrated circuits, etc.).

Southeast Asia continues to perform in MHPP as an important supplier of agricultural commodities (rice, natural rubber, spices, coconut, palm oil, etc.). Countries in the region (especially Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines) - among the world leaders in osbsyahamy investments.

South and East Asia are on separate cards. A separate group in Asia are the newly industrialized countries (NICs) of Southeast and East Asia. (South Korea, Singapore, Syanґan, Taiwan), and the country's second wave of industrialization '90 twentieth century. (Malaysia, Thailand, Brunei, Indonesia, Philippines). NEC was founded in economy open type of free economic zones and export-oriented industries. Over the past decade, significantly increased the share of industry in GDP continues to increase the share of the service sector, which indicates the approach of these countries to the developed category. Thus, the cause of economic take-off NIK Southeast and East Asia began restructuring the economy with the widespread introduction of scientific and technological progress.
