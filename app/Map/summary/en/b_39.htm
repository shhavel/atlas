The Nature Conservancy - a comprehensive system of international, national and community measures aimed at conservation, sustainable use and restoration of natural resources, including the conservation of species diversity (gene pool) flora and fauna of the earth and its mineral and water resources, air and respectively, to preserve the natural conditions of human society.

In order to protect nature carried out the following activities:

  • establishment of protected areas;
  • regulation of natural resources;
  • protection of the environment (water protection measures, land reclamation, regulation of runoff, reducing the volume of waste water and its purification, etc.);
  • independent environmental assessment of projects and assessment of the impact of economic activity on the environment;
  • monitoring of the environment (from regional to global monitoring of the biosphere).

Protected areas, depending on the purpose, features conservation status and size are divided into reserves, national parks, wildlife sanctuaries, reserves, monuments and other protected areas. The total area of ​​protected areas in the world is more than 5% of the land. The most common in the world are national parks, preserves and reserves.

Reserves - a plot of land or water areas permanently withdrawn from economic use; their main purpose - to serve as a model of nature.

Reserve - a territory which over the years in certain seasons or year round are protected species of animals, plants, or the share of natural complex.

National Parks - the most common form of protected areas, which are characterized by a massive admission of visitors to a certain part of the territory.
