Basic concepts and terms

Ancient Slavs - a group of tribes belonging to the Indo-European community - ancestors of the Slavic peoples of Eastern, Central and Southern Europe.

The Slavs - a large group of European nations united by common language and proximity origin. In the default language regarding the division of the eastern (Ukrainian, Belarusian, Russian), western (Poles, Czechs, Slovaks, Sorbs) and southern (Bulgarians, Macedonians, Croats, Slovenes, Bosnians, Montenegrins, Serbs). Slavic languages ​​belong to the Indo-European language family.

Migration - grand ethnic migration, which took place in Europe at 4.6 cent. Caused by Hun invasion Migration caused the formation of a series of barbarian kingdoms - Goths, Vandals, Burgundians, Franks, Lombards and others. A large settlement of peoples led to the so-called Great settlement of the Slavs.

A large settlement of the Slavs took place in Europe in the 6-7 cent., When Slavic tribes appear in the Danube, in the deep areas of the Balkans - in the south, west to reach the Elbe and the Baltic coast; the northeast occupy the top of the Don and Volga. Due to the large settlement of the Slavs was initiated separation Slavic ethnic group that initiated the formation of the Slavic peoples.

Byzantium, the Byzantine Empire - the state that existed in the eastern part of the Roman Empire from the end of 4 to mid-15th century. Got its name from the ancient colony on the shores of the Bosphorus - Byzantium, the site of the emperor Constantine in 330. Established the state capital - Constantinople. Byzantium was considered the successor to the Roman Empire, as the Byzantines called themselves Romans - "Romeo" and their country "romeyskoyu." The official language in Article 4.6. was Latin, and Greek.

Gauthier - the general name of ethno-political union of Germanic tribes first half of the first millennium AD. Immigrants from Scandinavia, which at the beginning of our era, migrated to the Baltic states, and later moved to the southeast up to the Black and Azov Seas. It created a tribal alliance, the so-called Gothic State defeated the Huns in the 5th century.

Huns - Turkic-speaking people who prykochuvav in the steppes of the Ukraine territory of Mongolia and China, replacing Iranian nomads, and opened the era of the Great Migration, during which largely formed modern ethnic map of Europe.

Avars - a great alliance of nomadic Turkic tribes. In the 6th century. came from Asia through Ukrainian territory on the Danube and established their own state Avar khanate.

Venedi - ancient Slavic name under which they are mentioned in ancient sources and Art. and the oldest Slavic etnopleminne. At that time, the Veneti occupied the land between the north of the Pripyat, Vistula in the west and south of Middle TN, without a clear definition of the eastern borders.

Anti - etnopleminne ancient Slavs, which occupied the territory between the Dniester and Dnieper. Archaeological Ants are penkivska culture. In the 6th century. anti with other Slavic tribes were actively involved in the colonization of the Balkans, the Slavs in wars against Byzantium. Last record of 602 dates from Ants. Sow winter crops taught in the second half of the 1 thousand.

5. Slavs during the Great Migration

Settling Ants and sklavyniv occurred at a time known in history as the Great Migration. Over by him, the Slavs were actively involved in military events, gaining experience in public life, which subsequently implemented in their own countries.

Under the rule of the Goths and Huns

In the 3rd century. Goths finally established in the Black Sea steppes. Their state united many nations of those lands, including Slavs, Antes.

Gothic domination fell during the invasion of the Huns. With the support of anti overcame the Goths.

As part of Hun association Slavs making a way to the Danube and the Balkans, acting then as allies nomads, as their subordinates.

The greatest power of the Huns under Attila at the time of, and after his death in 453. Fast weak.

Freed depending on the Huns, Slavs powerful stream of earth moved to Byzantium. Thus began large settlement of the Slavs.
