On the captured German Nazis Ukrainian territories unfolded Resistance Movement, which played a major role in the defeat of the invaders. In early 1943 there was a turning point in the war, and Soviet troops entered the territory of Ukraine. Never before has our land been the scene of fierce battles. Important for the liberation of the Ukrainian lands was the Battle of the Dnieper.

November 6 was released Kyiv. In December 1943 began a general offensive of the Soviet troops on the Right Bank Ukraine. In October 1944 the entire territory of Ukraine was liberated from the invaders. The war cost the Ukrainian nation of about 8 million lives. The memory of the participants in those bloody events commemorated October 14 - the day of the liberation of Ukraine and May 9 - the day that millions of people is a celebration of victory.
