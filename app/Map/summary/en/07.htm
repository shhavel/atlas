The geological structure of the territory of Ukraine is the result of complex processes, which lasted for many geological eras and periods - from the ancient (Archaean) to the present (Quaternary). Different conditions led to the formation characteristics of individual parts of our country. To better understand the patterns of formation and occurrence of rocks to apply the legend card which is composed of a geochronological table. Determining the age of rocks makes it possible to determine the sequence of geological processes that affect the formation of relief.
