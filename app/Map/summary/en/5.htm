Soils Ukraine

Soil - a loose top layer of soil that is composed of humus, sand, clay, water, air and minerals and plants grow there. This is the link that connects the living and inanimate nature.

The formation of soils associated with climate, vegetation, topography, activity of microorganisms, mineral composition of the ground (bedrock) and labor rights.

Soil that contains a lot of humus and is black called black soil.

The soil in which many sand called sand, and one in which a significant content of clay - clay.

The color depends on soil compounds that are part of it.

Soil fertility - the ability to provide the plants with moisture, air and nutrients. It is determined by the quantity and quality of humus.

Soil is the most expensive richness of humanity. Black soil, the most fertile in the world, covered two-thirds of Ukraine.
