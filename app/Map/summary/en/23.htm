Landscapes (or natural-territorial complexes) - various sized plot with a certain uniformity of physical and geographical conditions. These areas have been caused by long interconnected and interdependent nature of the components in certain areas. Natural landscapes are composed of natural ingredients, rocks, air, water, soil, living organisms. The interaction between them is caused by physical and geographical landscape-effects.

Comprehensive studies of landscapes need their synthesis, the combination of size, features of the natural environment and other components. Such designation and description of large areas, which are inherent similarities of nature, called physical-geographic zone.
