Polish-Cossack armed conflict in 1625

Concerned about the massive Ukrainian pokozachennyam that implementing their own government, refused to obey the authorities, the Commonwealth government began preparing for another punitive campaign against the Cossacks.

In September 1625 the great crown hetman Stanislaw Konetspolskiy of 30-thousand troops went to the Bar on Podniprov`ya.

Cossacks, headed by Mark Zhmaylom met Poles near Cannes, successfully fought back.

Particularly significant losses suffered by the Polish army Lake Kurukove.

This forced the Polish commanders to resort to peace talks that have been completed entering into a compromise agreement. From Cossacks Kurukivsku agreement signed Michael Doroshenko, Hetman elected instead Zhmayla.

Kurukivska Agreement

The agreement provided:

  • Cossack register up to 6 thousand .;
  • Those who are not included in the list, had to return to their owners' estates;
  • one thousand Cossacks had to constantly be in Zaporozhye, to prevent migration of peasants.
  • Cossacks were ordered, not to interfere in religious matters; abandon sea voyages; not maintain relations with other states.
  • Kurukivska agreement establishes a clear division of the Registered Cossacks on a territorial basis.
  • It was created six regiments Registered: Bila, Kanev, Korsun, Pereyaslav, Cherkasy and Chyhyryn a thousand soldiers each. Each regiment consisted of ten hundred.
  • Military power over Registered belonged older (Hetman), who chose the Combined Board and approved the Polish government.

REBELLION 1630 led Taras Fedorovich (Tryasylo)

The causes of the revolt

Kurukivska agreement intensified the contradiction between the Cossacks - those who went to the registry and those who remained outside. What was the most recent, the threat of new outbreaks Cossack resentment remained.

Moreover, the situation in Ukraine is complicated by the introduction of a new state tax. Not abate conflicts on religious grounds.

In order to sustain the population in check, the Polish government resorted to force, to Kyiv region sent a significant portion of the Crown forces. But these reckless actions only worsened the situation.

Residents of towns and villages were taken up arms. Tens of thousands of refugees gathered on the Host. Yes matured a new uprising.

Course uprising

Nereyestrovtsi elected hetman Taras Fedorovich, better known by the nickname Tryasylo.

In March 1630 he and his army came to Cherkassy, ​​and then moved to Korsun, after receipt by the side of the majority Registered F. passed.

The Polish government to suppress the rebellion sent a punitive expedition Crown army.

Decisive battles between rebels and the Polish army occurred during Pereyaslav. They lasted for three weeks. May 20, 1630 Cossacks staged punitive "Tarasov night."

Pereyaslav agreement in 1630

The reasons for the conclusion:

  • successful insurgency;
  • rumors of diplomatic contacts with Moscow Cossacks, Crimea and Sweden;
  • dissatisfaction Polish mercenaries who were detained salary.

REBELLION 1635 headed by Ivan Sulima

The causes of the revolt

During the 1632-1634 biennium. Thousand Cossacks took part in the Polish-Muscovite (Smolensk) war. When in 1634 between Moscow and Warsaw signed Polyanovskyy peace treaty, the Polish government once again resorted to trying to eliminate the Cossacks. Previously Poles prompted continuing complaints Cossacks governments of Turkey and the Crimea. After hiking Cossack relations between the Commonwealth and these countries are so strained that threatened war. In February 1635 the Commonwealth Sejm approved a special resolution "On termination of the Cossack tyranny" on territory of the Dnieper were put troops of the Crown forces.

In addition, in order to control the Cossacks expected to build in the lower Dnieper, closer to the Sich, a strong fortress, which could be permanently Polish garrison. The soldiers of the garrison would hinder fugitives who sought to get to the bottom, and cut off the main supply routes Zaporozhye food and ammunition.

Construction of the Kodak Fortress

Decided to build a fortress on the right bank near the first threshold - Kodatska.

Construction led the French military engineer, mercenary Polish King Guillaume Levasseur de Boplan.

Kodak fortress built quickly: construction work lasted four months, and by mid-summer 1635, she was quite ready.

In Kodak placed me Polish garrison, commanded by a French officer Marion.

Obtaining Kodak Cossacks

Construction of Kodak is extremely angered Cossacks. They elected Hetman Sula, who developed the plan, sending destruction Kodak universal calling to fight. In early August 1635 the Cossack army issued a Host.

Ivan Sulima took advantage of the absence of the crown forces and Registered who then fought in the Baltic. The Cossacks easily captured and partially destroyed Kodak fortress. After completing the operation the Cossacks went to Zaporozhye, where one of the islands built camp.

In late August launched a campaign against the Cossacks.

The situation was complicated by the rebels. Poholodnishalo, enough for food and fuel. The camp grew discontent. This took advantage of traitors, they took Cossack leader and his closest aides and gave them the Poles.

National liberation uprising 1637-1638 biennium. PAVLYUK led PAUL, James Ostrainyn, Dmytro Gouna

The brutal massacre of Sulyma and his devotees by itself was not able to suppress the Cossack movement.

Therefore, the government decided to revise the Commonwealth Register of Cossack Army and remove from it all unreliable. Doing the Polish magnates were the reason for a new uprising.

It was headed by Colonel Paul Registered Pawluk (Genesis).

The main battles between warring parties held December 6, 1637 at Kumeykamy. The rebels were defeated.

"Ordination ..." 1638

March 1, 1638 in Warsaw started working parliament, which passed a resolution entitled "ordination Registered Cossack Army, who is in the service of the Commonwealth."

It was about the need to use military force against the rebels.

"In perpetuity" Cossacks were deprived of the right to elect officers.

Forbidden as Cossack proceedings.

Registered army was limited to 6 thousand. Cossacks.

Without the permission of the government's High Commissioner in danger of death, no one was allowed to go to Zaporozhye.

The Cossacks were not allowed to settle in the cities, in addition to border - Cherkasy, Chyhyryn and Korsun.

Two shelves Registered, assuming each one were constantly stand in Zaporozhye.

New stage revolt

At the end of March 1638 several detachments of Cossacks, led by James Ostrainyn, Dmitry Guney and Carp throwing themselves from a Host and moved to Kyiv and Poltava region. Road to the Sich joined numerous militias peasants and townspeople.

The rebels decided to stop at the town Hovtvoyu. May 1, 1638 there occurred a victorious battle with the Poles.

So under the authority of the rebels was all Poltava. To finally break the enemy army led by Ostrainyn Lubni but Lubny siege was unsuccessful. Having suffered numerous losses, the rebels retreated to Zhovnyna and there were camp.

When Zhovnynom (May 31 - June 10, 1638) again battle occurred as a result of which rebel forces were scattered. Heavily wounded Carp Skydan was captured and was probably executed.

Ostrainyn of Cossack cavalry was cut off from the camp, and decided that the battle was lost, with the Cossacks crossed the frontier of Muscovy future land Slobozhanshchina.

The rebels, who remained in the camp (about 20 thousand.) Was elected Hetman Dmytro Hunyu and continued to fight. On the night of June 10 Hun organized retreat 10 thousand. Cossacks. Above the old Dnieper rivers, in the tract The elder, the rebels have laid a new camp.

About two months (June-July) Cossacks stubbornly fought the enemy. However, the situation in the camp deteriorated. Not the best was the case in Poland. Command both sides decided to resort to negotiation. Soon the Cossack officers accepted the terms offered by the Polish government. Among them was based on the "ordination Cossack Army ...".

LOCATION uprisings 20-30's. XVII. IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF UKRAINIAN NATIONAL LIBERATION MOVEMENT

Cossacks as a state of the Polish government to liquidate failed and, therefore, were in vain hope that the Cossacks won a huge military experience zazhyvshy European glory unsurpassed valor, zahartuvavshys in numerous military competition on home soil, voluntarily renounce desire to win will.

Rise of the 30's. Despite their spontaneity and incomplete, it was shown that the Cossacks were a leading group of Ukrainian society was supported by the entire population, and its action in the fight for the rights of every year become more organized and focused.

With this in mind, again seems particularly uprising 1637-1638 biennium. Among others it was different and scope of military operations, and the number of participants and duration.

He had a speech Cossack another great feature: the first time its leaders appealed to the entire population of Ukraine to support the Cossacks. As a versatile, letters and edicts clearly defined aim of the struggle, to liberate Ukraine from the yoke of the Commonwealth, to get rid of the national-religious oppression of others.

The rebellion was defeated because its leaders launched the program has not become nationwide. This spontaneity, lack of proper cohesion, dispersion forces and their rebel combatants - those flaws through which the Cossack and peasant movements 30's. No success. Association of Ukrainian liberation struggle around the idea took time. So the decade "golden peace" 1638-1648 g. For Ukraine was sometimes cohesion and accumulation of forces of the new race.
