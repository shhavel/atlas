TERROR IN UKRAINE AFTER Battle of Poltava

After the Battle of Poltava Ukraine wave king of terror.

Punitive measures related to all aspects of life and have diverse expression - from the death penalty ban Mazepas to print books in Ukrainian.

Many in the administration were deprived estates and government posts.

Instead, the important positions were given to officials not to Ukrainian patriots, and with Russians, Germans, etc., and one Ukrainian officers who maintained loyalty to the king.

Many officers were arrested and exiled to Siberia. Russia and sent to family Mazepas, and arranged themselves to hunt all over Europe.

For example, a nephew of Andrew Mazepa Voinarovsky was arrested in Hamburg in October 1716

The same fate befell Mazepa General Hertsyka captain Gregory, who was arrested in Warsaw.

Countries of Europe Moscow agents scoured in search of Philip Orlik and his family.

After the Battle of Poltava Moscow troops behaved in the Ukrainian lands as conquered country. From their tyranny suffered not only supporters of the Hetman, but also civilians.

Extremely difficult test for Ukrainian steel forced channel, the construction of fortifications, military "grassroots" campaigns and more. Duzhyh and rich peasants often sent to St. Petersburg, Astrakhan, the Caucasus, using cheap labor.

Thus, in 1716 the construction of the Volga-Don canal near Tsaritsyn sent 10 thousand. Cossacks.

In 1718 a new department here sent Ukrainian troops. Another division that year built a fortified line above,. Terek in the Caucasus.

In 1721 10 thousand. Cossacks were sent to construction of the Ladoga Canal, and in 1722 they took the new 10 thousand. Cossacks.

The number of those who come from such campaigns, ranged from 30 to 60%, the rest of the Cossacks and peasants died of unbearable living conditions, epidemics, injury, etc.

Thus, the tsarist government led to the extinction of tens of thousands of Ukrainian.

After the Battle of Poltava significantly increased attack and Ukrainian statehood. In addition to direct pressure, the Russian administration to have a pretext for interference in Ukrainian affairs, provoked hostility among officers and Hetman, between officers and farmers, etc., sought to create the impression that all evil comes from the Hetman and officers, and all is well - from Moscow.

Hetman Ivan Skoropadskiy. RESHETYLIVSKI ARTICLES

Hetman Ivan Skoropadskiy (1708-1722 gg.), According to tradition, was launched with the signing of papers.

But Peter refused to do so, explaining his decision to war circumstances.

After the Battle of Poltava, July 17, Skoropadskiy while the Cossack army in camp under Reshetilovka, appealed to the king of the 14 paragraphs of articles.

In them, he requested to confirm the rights and liberties Hetman and solve many important matters of everyday life.

In response, Peter sent a nominal decree, which does not like the interstate treaty article.

In the royal decree rights and liberties confirmed in such a way that meet the interests of Muscovy. Subsequently promised dates and articles.

As to other matters, the king said that during a campaign under the command of Russian generals have to pass not only simple Cossacks, and even the hetman.

Governor inhabit Peter testified in Ukrainian cities, as before; in the Ukrainian case they do not interfere, except for the "state" as "treason" (for "treason" were understood any action going against the wishes of the king).

An important innovation was the appointment of a resident Izmailov royal hetman. He was given the right to exercise control over the Hetman and the Government of Ukraine.

Peculiar attachment to the decree were overt and covert article Hour.

These items are called ground troops Zaporizhzhya "Little Russian land", which together with the resident royal hetman had to hold the entire population, including rebels, Cossacks, "in silence and obedience to the Great State"

Nakazuvalosya also "svoyevoltsiv eliminate" do not allow them to settle in one place, especially on the Host. Foreign envoys Hetman had to make only with Being. Last obliged immediately to inform the king about the visits and calls and send letters brought by ambassadors.

In addition, residents had to watch to the Hetman without permission of the king did not alter the structure of the military posts in the General Office and not appointed new colonels took away and do not give anyone the estates.

Resident duty was to Hetman government informed the king of all their income.

Residence was appointed Hetman deaf, which was located almost on the border of Moscow.

Reported that when the hetman maintained two Russian regiments, however, will be a royal resident.

So no confirmation on which ancient rights and liberties in the decree of Peter I was. On the contrary, the measures introduced, turned the once autonomous Ukrainian state on the typical "suburb of Muscovy."

Measures aimed at undermining ECONOMY Hetmanate

The imperial government has set for itself the main goal: to save the Hetmanate economic independence, to subordinate its economic life of Russia's economy and turn to a reliable source of income. King's activities primarily related to trade.

At the beginning of the XVIII century. Ukraine has traditionally maintained close trade ties with Central and Western Europe. Peter King sought to block his decrees significant trade routes, forcing the Ukrainian merchant rolls back in.

Such prohibitions and restrictions used the Russian government and the importation of foreign goods into Ukraine. These measures have had to make Ukrainian instead of buying foreign products manufactory young Muscovite.

Inhibited the development of Russian trade and customs policy, set of Russian-Ukrainian border duty not used for the enrichment of Ukrainian state treasury, and to Moscow.

One of the measures that hindered the development of the Ukrainian economy, the Russian government was to sell the Ukrainian territory copper money to gold and silver in circulation remained mostly population, enriched by the Russian treasury.

Ruining the economy of Ukraine permanent residence in its territory a great Russian army (10 thousand.) That was maintained at the expense of the Ukrainian population.

To finally conquer Ukrainian, Peter struck education and typography, trying to keep the activity of such powerful cultural centers like Kiev-Mohyla Academy, Kyiv-Pechersk Lavra.

In 1709, Peter I ordered the Kiev governor send abroad all "Polish" (ie right-origin) students and report how many students will remain "Little Russian" - natives of Hetman, as well as monks are teachers from the right bank.

Dramatically worsened the situation of the Church of the Kyiv Metropolis. When in 1718 he died Kyiv Metropolitan, the Russian government banned new vote. In 1722 the first hierarchs of the Kyiv Metropolis was given the title of Archbishop of Kiev and Little Russia. Assign it had a church synod led by the king in St. Petersburg.

So, Kiev Metropolis into a regular diocese of the Russian Orthodox Church.

Destruction of Ukrainian culture performed by such devious actions as poaching Ukrainian scientists, theologians, writers and teachers from Kyiv to St. Petersburg and Moscow.

FORMATION Little Russian College

Evidence of complete destruction of the state of the autonomy of Ukraine was the creation in April 1722 of the Little Russian Collegium.

The decree was announced April 29, 1722

It reported on a Little Russian Collegium of six officers of the Russian regiments stationed in Ukraine, led by foreman Velyaminovym.

Hetman show of obedience Ministry of Foreign Affairs and subordinate to the Senate as an ordinary province of the empire.

Brigadier Velyaminov arrived Glukhova July 21, 1722 at led by the Little Russian Collegium relied very wide powers.

Board took over all administrative leadership of the Left-Bank Ukraine.

Members of the Little Russian Collegium had to supervise the activities of the Hetman, general and regimental officers.

They controlled the establishment and collection of taxes to the royal treasury, quartering of Russian soldiers and officers in Ukraine.

Little Russian College oversaw the activities of the General Military Chancellery.

Russian officials followed the distribution of landholdings among officers.

However, the Little Russian Collegium was higher appellate authority.

Also Velyaminov had discretion to intervene in any sphere of Ukrainian lives as full owner of the land.

Causes Recovery Hetman

In the summer of 1727 increased tension in Russian-Turkish relations. Russia started preparations for a new war. The government of Peter II, relying on military power Cossacks sought to win over Cossack officers.

It eliminated the Little Russian Collegium and allowed elections hetman.

Not least in making that decision played a personal interest Menshikov - the person leading the court for a minor Peter II.

Menshikov hoped in this way to expand the already vast estates in Ukraine.

In this situation, the sole and undisputed candidate to the hetman was Danylo Apostol.

In the elections, held on October 1 in Deaf and thrown off the ceremony rather oath in 73-year-old Apostle voted unanimously. Contracting articles, as well as from Skoropadskim, was signed.

Thus, its main business Apostle saw the signing of a traditional Russian-Ukrainian agreement.

To this end, the newly elected Hetman decided to take a trip to St. Petersburg for the coronation of Peter II.

In the Russian capital Ukrainian hetman put great efforts to rectify the matter, submitted written petitions, met with influential dignitaries.

In early March, the Apostle Daniel began talks with the president of the Foreign Board of Count Golovkin, gave him a prepared paper. Only in August Hetman received in response to their proposals "Rishytelni points." They had a form of imperial government decree hetman.

"Rishytelni points" in 1728

According to the "new contract" Hetman deprived of the right to negotiate with other countries.

Only allowed to solve border problems with Poland and the Crimea, but under the supervision of the Russian resident.

The military governments were elected by the people of the house, however they are approved by the emperor.

Lower officers claimed hetman. The highest judicial body became General Court.

It consisted of three Russians and three Ukrainian and led by Hetman, but "chief judge" was considered the king.

Lifted ban Russians to buy land in Ukraine, although it noted that the new landowners obey the Ukrainian administration.

New Articles considerably restrict the already curtailed the rights and liberties of Ukraine.

As for Hetman Apostol, the rights granted to him even less than Skoropadsky.

Thus, "Rishytelni items" were not bilateral agreement. They had the appearance of order, regulation supreme power of the Russian Empire. The document was intended to regulate the internal life Hetmanate as part of a single state.

Reform Danylo Apostol

Given the realities of Apostle Daniel started to implement reforms aimed at streamlining the internal life of Hetman.

Reforms conducted in the areas of justice, finance, land affairs, trade and so on.

With a view to resolving the land of the Apostle during 1729-1731 years. Held a general investigation of the estates. As a result of this event in the state was facing a lot of land, arbitrarily seized from private ownership.

Many efforts have been made to revitalize the Apostle Ukrainian trade. Getman was able to reverse many restrictions and harassment, introduced by Peter I. For example, with the assistance of Daniel Apostle Ukrainian merchants back right to export goods to Western markets; was again established trade relations with the Crimea and more.

Pursuing reform of Finance, the Apostle first installed the exact budget of public expenditure; while among the expenditures singled out the section that was used for the maintenance of Cossack administration hired troops, artillery and more. Getman also identified ways of filling the state treasury Hetman.

Changes in legal proceedings related to reforming the courts, the introduction of mandatory documents, reduce to a collection of laws that were used in the Ukrainian judiciary. Regimental courts were divided into two types: for business critical and criminal (ie for crimes) and for small cases. Centesimal and rural courts turned to peer (shared, common).

In addition, overcoming the resistance of the Moscow government, the Apostle restored Hetman right to appoint general officers and colonels.

By Hetman Apostol could significantly reduce the number of Russians in the Ukrainian administration. Was limited to six regiments of Russian in Ukraine.

Thus, the Apostle Daniel managed to temporarily slow down the process of converting Hetmanate administrative unit of the Russian Empire, at least for a while, the rights and freedoms that have remained in Ukrainian.
